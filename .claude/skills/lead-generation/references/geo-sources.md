# Geographic Sources — Per-Country Registries & Data Sources

## Overview

This reference catalogs the primary registries, databases, and data sources for company and contact research across the Netherlands, Belgium, and Luxembourg. Sources are organized by country, then by type.

---

## Netherlands (NL)

### Company Registries

**KVK (Kamer van Koophandel) — Chamber of Commerce**
- URL: kvk.nl
- What: Company register (Handelsregister); all Dutch legal entities
- Data: Legal name, trading name, address, SBI code, registration date, authorized signatories, UBO register
- Access: Basic search free; detailed extracts paid (kvk.nl/zoeken)
- Use: Company verification, corporate structure, director identification
- Note: KVK number is the unique identifier for Dutch companies; always capture it

**CBS (Centraal Bureau voor de Statistiek) — Statistics Netherlands**
- URL: cbs.nl / opendata.cbs.nl
- What: National statistics office
- Data: Sector-level revenue, employment, production statistics; no individual company data
- Access: Free via StatLine (opendata.cbs.nl)
- Use: Sector sizing, benchmarking, estimating averages when company-specific data unavailable
- Key datasets: "Bedrijven; bedrijfstak" (companies by sector), healthcare statistics, agricultural census

**Jaarrekeningen (Annual Accounts)**
- Filed with KVK; accessible via kvk.nl or third-party services
- Large companies (groot) must file full financials; medium companies file abbreviated; small companies file limited
- Also available via: Company.info, Dun & Bradstreet Netherlands, Graydon

### Financial Sector

**DNB (De Nederlandsche Bank)**
- URL: dnb.nl
- What: Central bank and prudential supervisor
- Data: Register of supervised institutions (banks, insurers, pension funds, payment institutions)
- Access: Public register at dnb.nl/toezichtprofessioneel/registers
- Use: Comprehensive list of all licensed financial institutions in NL

**AFM (Autoriteit Financiele Markten)**
- URL: afm.nl
- What: Financial markets conduct authority
- Data: Register of licensed investment firms, advisors, auditors
- Access: Public register at afm.nl/registers
- Use: Investment firms, financial advisors, audit firms

### Healthcare

**NZa (Nederlandse Zorgautoriteit)**
- URL: nza.nl
- What: Healthcare market regulator
- Data: Financial data for all healthcare providers, market analyses, tariff decisions
- Access: Open data at nza.nl/open-data
- Use: Revenue rankings, financial benchmarking for hospitals and care providers

**Jaarverslagenzorg.nl**
- URL: jaarverslagenzorg.nl
- What: Healthcare annual reports repository
- Data: Mandatory annual reports for healthcare institutions
- Access: Free search and download
- Use: Detailed financial and organizational data for healthcare entities

**CIBG**
- URL: cibg.nl
- Data: BIG-register (healthcare professionals), AGB-register (healthcare organizations)
- Use: Organization identification, professional verification

### Agriculture & Horticulture

**RVO (Rijksdienst voor Ondernemend Nederland)**
- URL: rvo.nl
- What: Netherlands Enterprise Agency
- Data: Agricultural subsidies, farm registrations, energy subsidies
- Use: Farm sizing, subsidy recipients (proxy for scale)

**Glastuinbouw Nederland**
- URL: glastuinbouwnederland.nl
- What: Greenhouse horticulture industry association
- Data: Member directory, sector statistics
- Use: Primary source for greenhouse companies

**Royal FloraHolland**
- URL: royalfloraholland.com
- What: Cooperative flower auction (largest in the world)
- Data: Annual report with member data, auction turnover by category
- Use: Grower identification, sector sizing

### Logistics & Transport

**TLN (Transport en Logistiek Nederland)**
- URL: tln.nl
- What: Largest Dutch transport and logistics association (~5,000 members)
- Data: Member directory, sector reports
- Use: Company identification, sector sizing

**NIWO (Nationale en Internationale Wegvervoer Organisatie)**
- URL: niwo.nl
- What: Issuer of road transport licenses (Eurovergunning)
- Data: Licensed road transport operators
- Use: Fleet sizing proxy, operator identification

**Port of Rotterdam**
- URL: portofrotterdam.com
- What: Europe's largest port
- Data: Tenant directory, operator information, cargo statistics
- Use: Logistics company identification, port-related companies

**Port of Amsterdam**
- URL: portofamsterdam.com
- Data: Operator directory, business directory
- Use: Similar to Rotterdam for Amsterdam-based logistics

### Defense & Government

**Defensie.nl**
- URL: defensie.nl
- What: Ministry of Defence
- Data: Organizational structure, procurement information
- Use: Understanding defense organizational buyers

**PIANOo (Expertisecentrum Aanbesteden)**
- URL: pianoo.nl
- What: Dutch public procurement expertise center
- Data: Procurement guidelines, tender insights, buyer profiles
- Use: Understanding government procurement processes

**TenderNed**
- URL: tenderned.nl
- What: National tender publication platform
- Data: All public procurement tenders above EU thresholds
- Use: Identifying government buyers, understanding procurement needs, finding defense contractors

**NIDV**
- URL: nidv.eu
- What: Netherlands Industry for Defence and Security
- Data: ~400 member companies in defense and security sector
- Use: Defense contractor identification

### General Business Rankings

**MT500 / MT1000**
- URL: mt.nl
- What: Management Team magazine's annual company rankings
- Data: Top 500/1000 Dutch companies by revenue
- Use: Cross-vertical company sizing and ranking

**Top500.nl / Elsevier Top 500**
- Data: Alternative Dutch company ranking by revenue
- Use: Cross-reference with MT500

**Computable.nl**
- URL: computable.nl
- What: Leading Dutch IT publication
- Data: IT decision-maker interviews, CIO profiles, technology adoption articles
- Use: Contact identification, technology adoption signals

## Belgium (BE)

### Company Registries

**KBO/BCE (Kruispuntbank van Ondernemingen / Banque-Carrefour des Entreprises)**
- URL: kbo.be / kbopub.economie.fgov.be
- What: Crossroads Bank for Enterprises; central Belgian company register
- Data: Company name, address, legal form, activities (NACE codes), active status
- Access: Free public search
- Use: Company verification, activity classification, director identification

**NBB/BNB (Nationale Bank van Belgie / Banque Nationale de Belgique)**
- URL: nbb.be
- What: Central bank; also hosts the company annual accounts database (Balanscentrale)
- Data: Annual accounts for all Belgian companies
- Access: Free via nbb.be/en/central-balance-sheet-office
- Use: Revenue, assets, employee data for Belgian companies

### Financial Sector

**FSMA (Financial Services and Markets Authority)**
- URL: fsma.be
- What: Financial markets regulator
- Data: Register of licensed financial institutions, investment firms, intermediaries
- Access: Public register
- Use: Comprehensive list of regulated financial entities in Belgium

**Febelfin**
- URL: febelfin.be
- What: Belgian financial sector federation
- Data: Member list (banks, payment institutions, financial institutions)
- Use: Banking and financial services company identification

**Assuralia**
- URL: assuralia.be
- What: Belgian insurance association
- Data: Member insurance companies
- Use: Insurance sector company identification

### Healthcare

**Zorgnet-Icuro**

- Data: Member directory, sector statistics

**Belfius Hospital Barometer**
- What: Annual financial benchmarking of Belgian hospitals
- Data: Revenue, financial health indicators for hospitals
- Use: Hospital ranking and sizing

### Agriculture

**Boerenbond**
- URL: boerenbond.be

- Data: Member information, sector reports

**VLAM (Vlaams Centrum voor Agro- en Visserijmarketing)**

- What: Flemish center for agri- and fisheries marketing
- Data: Sector statistics, producer directories
- Use: Agricultural sector sizing

### Logistics

**Port of Antwerp-Bruges**

- What: Europe's second-largest port (by tonnage)
- Data: Operator directory, tenant information, cargo statistics
- Use: Major logistics company identification

**VIL (Vlaams Instituut voor de Logistiek)**
- URL: vil.be
- What: Flemish Institute for Logistics
- Data: Member directory, sector research
- Use: Logistics company identification, innovation signals

**Febetra**
- URL: febetra.be
- What: Belgian road transport federation
- Data: Member directory
- Use: Transport company identification

### Defense

**Belgian Defence**
- URL: mil.be
- What: Belgian Ministry of Defence
- Data: Organizational structure, procurement information
- Use: Understanding Belgian defense buyers

**BSDI (Belgian Security and Defence Industry)**
- URL: bsdi.be
- What: Belgian defense industry association
- Data: Member directory
- Use: Defense contractor identification

### General Business Rankings

**Trends Top 100.000**
- URL: trendstop.knack.be
- What: Top 100,000 Belgian companies by various metrics
- Data: Revenue, profit, employees, growth rates
- Access: Some free access; full access subscription-based
- Use: Primary source for Belgian company ranking and sizing across all verticals

**Datanews (Knack)**
- URL: datanews.knack.be
- What: Belgian IT publication (comparable to Computable.nl)
- Data: CIO/CTO interviews, IT rankings
- Use: Contact identification, technology adoption signals

## Luxembourg (LU)

### Company Registries

**RCSL (Registre de Commerce et des Societes)**
- URL: rcsl.lu / lbr.lu
- What: Trade and Companies Register
- Data: Company registration, directors, authorized signatories, annual accounts
- Access: Free basic search; detailed extracts paid
- Use: Company verification, director identification

**Chambre de Commerce Luxembourg**
- URL: cc.lu
- What: Chamber of Commerce
- Data: Member directory, economic data, business services
- Use: Company identification, networking

### Financial Sector (Major LU Strength)

**CSSF (Commission de Surveillance du Secteur Financier)**
- URL: cssf.lu
- What: Primary financial regulator
- Data: Complete registers of all licensed entities: banks (~130), investment funds (~3,700), insurance companies (~270), management companies, PFS
- Access: Public registers at cssf.lu
- Use: THE primary source for Luxembourg financial sector targets; comprehensive and authoritative

**ALFI (Association of the Luxembourg Fund Industry)**
- URL: alfi.lu
- What: Fund industry association
- Data: Member directory (asset managers, fund administrators, depositaries, law firms)
- Use: Investment fund industry targeting

**ABBL (Association des Banques et Banquiers, Luxembourg)**
- URL: abbl.lu
- What: Banking association
- Data: Member list, sector statistics
- Use: Bank identification and sizing

**ACA (Association des Compagnies d'Assurances)**
- URL: aca.lu
- What: Insurance association
- Data: Member insurance companies
- Use: Insurance sector targeting

### Agriculture

**ASTA (Administration des Services Techniques de l'Agriculture)**
- URL: asta.etat.lu
- What: Agricultural technical services administration
- Data: Agricultural sector data, producer registers
- Use: Limited scope; LU agriculture is small (~2,000 farms)

**Chambre d'Agriculture**
- URL: lwk.lu
- What: Agricultural chamber
- Data: Member information
- Use: Agricultural sector overview

### Logistics

**CLC (Cluster for Logistics Luxembourg)**
- URL: clc.lu
- What: Logistics cluster organization
- Data: Member directory, sector reports
- Use: Logistics company identification

### Defense & Government

**Luxembourg Defence / Armee Luxembourgeoise**
- URL: armee.lu / gouvernement.lu
- Data: Limited military; focus on EU/NATO institutional presence
- Use: Small but strategic due to NATO/EU presence

**NSPA (NATO Support and Procurement Agency)**
- URL: nspa.nato.int
- What: NATO procurement agency headquartered in Capellen, Luxembourg
- Data: Procurement opportunities, organizational structure
- Use: Unique LU-specific defense opportunity; significant IT procurement

## Cross-Border Sources

### EU-Level
- **Eurostat**: EU-wide statistics (ec.europa.eu/eurostat)
- **EU public procurement (TED)**: ted.europa.eu — all EU tenders above threshold
- **EBA / EIOPA / ESMA**: EU financial regulators; supervised entity lists
- **ECB**: Supervised bank list (SSM)

### Multi-Country Business Directories
- **Dun & Bradstreet**: Company data across all three countries
- **Orbis (Bureau van Dijk / Moody's)**: Comprehensive company database (subscription)
- **Company.info**: Dutch and Belgian company data
- **Graydon**: Benelux credit data and company information

### Industry-Specific Cross-Border
- **EBF (European Banking Federation)**: Member banking associations across EU
- **Insurance Europe**: Pan-European insurance data
- **ESA (European Space Agency)**: ESTEC in Noordwijk (NL) — relevant for defense/space vertical
- **Benelux Office for Intellectual Property (BOIP)**: Cross-border IP data
