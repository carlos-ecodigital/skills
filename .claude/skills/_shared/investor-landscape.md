## 1. Investor Taxonomy

| Type | Check Size | Decision Speed | Focus | Typical Stage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Seed VC | EUR 0.5-5M | 2-6 weeks | Growth, team, market, moat | Pre-seed to Seed |
| Climate tech VC | EUR 0.5-5M | 3-8 weeks | Impact + returns, technology | Seed to Series B |
| Infrastructure fund | EUR 5-50M | 2-6 months | Cash yield, contracted revenue | Series A+ / PF |
| Family office | EUR 0.5-10M | 2-8 weeks | Values, relationship, terms | Any stage |
| Strategic investor | EUR 1-10M | 4-12 weeks | Market access, synergy | Series A+ |
| Angel / syndicate | EUR 25K-500K | 1-4 weeks | Founder quality, upside | Pre-seed to Seed |
| Government / DFI | EUR 1-10M | 3-12 months | Policy, jobs, sovereignty | Any stage |
| Pension / insurer | EUR 10-100M | 6-12 months | Yield, ESG, duration | Series A+ / PF |
