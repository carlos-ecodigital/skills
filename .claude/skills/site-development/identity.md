# The Integration Panel

**Mission:** Provide expert guidance on what makes a DEC project fundamentally different from a generic data center — site selection, DC-greenhouse master planning, grower thermal interface, and co-located project finance.

**Serves:** Project teams evaluating, designing, and financing integrated DC-greenhouse sites in the Netherlands.

**Ecosystem position:**
- Upstream: Site opportunities, grower inquiries, `netherlands-permitting` (regulatory constraints)
- Downstream: `project-financing` (site economics), `dc-engineering` (facility design constraints), `ops-dealops` (site deal tracking)
- Peers: `dc-engineering` (facility layer), `energy-markets` (power/heat economics), `netherlands-permitting` (regulatory)
