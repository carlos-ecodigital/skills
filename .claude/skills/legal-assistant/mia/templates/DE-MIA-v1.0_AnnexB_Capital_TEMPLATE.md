## Annex B — Capital Introductions

This Annex B is part of the Master Introduction Agreement dated [MIA_DATE] between [DE:LEGAL_NAME] (the "**Provider**") and [INTRODUCER_NAME] (the "**Introducer**") (together, the "**MIA**"). Capitalised terms not defined in this Annex B have the meanings given in the Master.

### B.1 Activation and integration

B.1.1 This Annex B is activated by the Parties' execution of this Annex alongside the Master.

B.1.2 The provisions of this Annex B take effect from [ANNEX_B_EFFECTIVE_DATE].

B.1.3 The provisions of the Master apply to and form part of this Annex B. In the event of conflict between this Annex B and the Master, this Annex B prevails to the extent strictly necessary to give effect to the regulatory safeguards in this Annex B; otherwise the Master prevails.

B.1.4 **Regulatory intent.** The Parties enter into this Annex B on the express basis that the Introducer's activities under this Annex B are limited to introducing Investors and do not constitute the provision of investment services, investment advice, brokerage, placement, or any other regulated activity under MiFID II, the Wet op het financieel toezicht (Wft), the FCA Handbook, the US Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the rules of FINMA, or any equivalent regime in another jurisdiction (the "**Applicable Financial Regulation**"). The provisions of this Annex B (in particular Cl. B.3, B.4, B.5, B.12, and B.13) are intended to keep the arrangement outside the regulatory perimeter and shall be construed accordingly.

### B.2 Definitions specific to Annex B

"**Applicable Financial Regulation**" has the meaning given in Cl. B.1.4.

"**Approved Investor**" means an Investor named on a written list submitted by the Introducer under Cl. B.6 and approved in writing by the Provider in accordance with Cl. B.6.

"**Capital Committed**" means the aggregate amount of capital committed by an Approved Investor to the Provider under a binding subscription, investment, or commitment instrument, expressed in EUR (with foreign-currency commitments converted at the European Central Bank reference rate prevailing on the date of commitment).

"**Investor**" means a third party introduced by the Introducer to the Provider with a view to that party committing capital to the Provider or to a fund, vehicle, or transaction sponsored by the Provider.

"**Named Investor List**" means the list of intended Investors submitted by the Introducer to the Provider under Cl. B.6.

"**Qualifying Capital Introduction**" has the meaning given in Cl. B.7.

### B.3 Permitted activities

B.3.1 The Introducer may, in respect of an Approved Investor, do only the following:
(a) provide the Provider's contact information to the Approved Investor;
(b) arrange an initial meeting between the Provider and the Approved Investor;
(c) share publicly available information about the Provider; and
(d) confirm factual, non-evaluative information about the Provider on request from the Approved Investor.

### B.4 Prohibited activities

B.4.1 The Introducer shall not, in respect of any Investor (whether or not an Approved Investor), do any of the following:
(a) provide investment advice or recommendations;
(b) solicit, promote, market, or offer the investment opportunity;
(c) negotiate investment terms on behalf of either Party;
(d) distribute the Provider's offering materials, financial models, term sheets, or other non-public information;
(e) hold, receive, or transmit funds or securities;
(f) make representations about expected returns, risk profiles, or the merits of any investment opportunity;
(g) act as agent, representative, broker, dealer, placement agent, or financial intermediary of or for the Provider in any investment discussion or transaction; or
(h) hold itself out as having any of the capacities listed in (g).

### B.5 Regulatory representations

B.5.1 The Introducer represents and warrants to the Provider that:
(a) the Introducer is not a regulated investment firm, broker-dealer, placement agent, or financial intermediary in any jurisdiction, and does not hold any licence, registration, or authorisation that would imply such status;
(b) the Introducer's activities under this Annex B are limited to the activities permitted by Cl. B.3 and do not constitute investment services or any other regulated activity under any Applicable Financial Regulation; and
(c) the Introducer will not undertake any of the activities prohibited by Cl. B.4.

B.5.2 The Provider represents and warrants to the Introducer that:
(a) the Provider has not engaged the Introducer as a placement agent, financial advisor, broker, dealer, or financial intermediary; and
(b) the Provider acknowledges that the Introducer's role under this Annex B is limited to effecting introductions and that the Provider will not characterise the Introducer otherwise to any Investor or third party.

B.5.3 The Parties acknowledge that the structure of this Annex B is intended to fall outside the regulatory perimeter of the Applicable Financial Regulation and undertake to restructure or suspend this Annex B in accordance with Cl. B.12 if regulatory circumstances change.

### B.6 Named Investor List

B.6.1 The Introducer shall provide to the Provider, in writing, a Named Investor List identifying each specific Investor the Introducer intends to introduce under this Annex B.

B.6.2 The Provider shall, within [APPROVAL_WINDOW] of receipt of a Named Investor List (or any update to it), notify the Introducer in writing of which Investors on the list are approved as Approved Investors and which are rejected. An Investor not approved within that period is deemed rejected.

B.6.3 Only Approved Investors qualify for a Fee under this Annex B.

B.6.4 An Investor with whom the Provider has had substantive contact within the [LOOKBACK_MONTHS] preceding the date of submission of the Named Investor List is excluded from approval, in accordance with Clause 7 of the Master (Pipeline Carve-Out).

B.6.5 The Introducer may submit updates to the Named Investor List on demand, with no fixed cadence. The Provider's [APPROVAL_WINDOW] response window applies to each update.

### B.7 Qualifying Capital Introduction

B.7.1 A "**Qualifying Capital Introduction**" arises when all of the following have occurred in respect of the same Approved Investor:
(a) the Approved Investor was on a Named Investor List approved under Cl. B.6 prior to the introduction;
(b) the first substantive meeting between the Provider and the Approved Investor has been held; and
(c) the Approved Investor has executed a binding subscription, investment, or commitment instrument and the relevant Capital Committed has been received by the Provider in cleared funds (or, where the commitment instrument calls capital in tranches, the first tranche has been received in cleared funds).

B.7.2 The Pipeline Carve-Out in Clause 7 of the Master applies to all Introductions under this Annex B. No Fee is due in respect of an Investor that falls within Clause 7.1 of the Master.

### B.8 Fee schedule (Option 2 — Capped %)

B.8.1 The Fee for each Qualifying Capital Introduction is:
**2% of the Capital Committed**, capped at a maximum of **EUR 250,000** per Qualifying Capital Introduction.

B.8.2 Where Capital Committed is called in tranches, the Fee accrues pro rata as each tranche is received in cleared funds, subject to the EUR 250,000 cap applying in aggregate across all tranches relating to the same Qualifying Capital Introduction.

B.8.3 The Fee is calculated on Capital Committed, not on assets under management, returns realised, or any other metric.

### B.9 Payment

B.9.1 **Per-execution detail.** Currency, invoicing entity, payable-to entity, payment terms, and VAT/withholding treatment for each Qualifying Capital Introduction are set out in Schedule B-1. Default position: EUR, Net 30, VAT treatment per the recipient jurisdiction's reverse-charge rules where applicable.

B.9.2 **No refund of paid Fees.** Fees properly paid under this Annex B prior to suspension or termination of this Annex B (whether under Cl. B.11, B.12, or B.13) are non-refundable, save for the clawback expressly provided in Cl. B.11.2.

### B.10 Tail

B.10.1 The Introducer's right to receive a Fee in respect of an Approved Investor (and the Provider's obligation to pay it) survives termination of this Annex B (and of the Master) for any Qualifying Capital Introduction where conditions Cl. B.7.1(a) and (b) are satisfied within the period ending [ANNEX_B_TAIL] after termination.

B.10.2 No Fee accrues under this Annex B in respect of an introduction made for the first time more than [ANNEX_B_TAIL] after termination.

### B.11 Termination for cause and clawback

B.11.1 The Provider may terminate this Annex B immediately on written notice to the Introducer if the Introducer breaches any of Cl. B.3 (Permitted Activities), Cl. B.4 (Prohibited Activities), or Cl. B.5 (Regulatory Representations).

B.11.2 On termination under Cl. B.11.1, the Provider is entitled to claw back from the Introducer any Fees paid by the Provider under this Annex B in the twelve (12) months preceding the date of the breach event giving rise to termination. The Introducer shall pay the clawback amount to the Provider within thirty (30) days of written demand.

B.11.3 Termination of this Annex B under this Cl. B.11 does not, of itself, terminate the Master or Annex A.

### B.12 Automatic suspension on regulatory change

B.12.1 If any of the following events occurs (each a "**Regulatory Trigger**"):
(a) any regulator (including but not limited to AFM, FCA, SEC, FINMA, or any equivalent authority in another jurisdiction) issues a written letter, notice, guidance, or determination to or affecting either Party or this Annex B;
(b) any such regulator publishes formal guidance that, on a reasonable interpretation, would cause the activities permitted by Cl. B.3 to require a licence, registration, or authorisation that the Introducer does not hold;
(c) any such regulator brings, threatens, or settles enforcement action in respect of an arrangement materially comparable to this Annex B; or
(d) any legislative change is enacted that, on its effective date, would have either of the consequences in (a) or (b),

then this Annex B is automatically suspended with effect from the date of the Regulatory Trigger.

B.12.2 **Notice.** Either Party may, on becoming aware of a Regulatory Trigger, give written notice to the other Party identifying the Regulatory Trigger and confirming suspension of this Annex B. Failure to give notice does not prevent suspension under Cl. B.12.1; suspension takes effect on the date of the Regulatory Trigger irrespective of notice.

B.12.3 **Effect of suspension.** During suspension:
(a) no new introductions made under this Annex B trigger Fees;
(b) no new Investors are added to the Named Investor List;
(c) Fees that have already accrued in respect of Qualifying Capital Introductions completed prior to the Regulatory Trigger remain payable; and
(d) the Master and Annex A are unaffected and continue in full force.

B.12.4 **Restructure or terminate.** Within [SUSPENSION_RESTRUCTURE_WINDOW] of the date of suspension, the Parties shall negotiate in good faith either (a) a restructured Annex B that addresses the Regulatory Trigger and is compliant with the Applicable Financial Regulation, or (b) the termination of this Annex B. If the Parties do not agree a restructure or termination within that window, this Annex B terminates automatically at the end of that window.

### B.13 Enhanced severability

B.13.1 If any provision of this Annex B is held by any court, regulator, or competent authority to constitute a regulated activity that the Introducer is not licensed to conduct, this Annex B terminates automatically with effect from the date of that determination, without affecting the Master or Annex A.

B.13.2 Fees properly paid under this Annex B prior to such termination are non-refundable, subject only to Cl. B.11.2.

B.13.3 Clauses 5 (Confidentiality), 6 (Non-Circumvention), 7 (Pipeline Carve-Out), and 8 (General Provisions) of the Master continue to apply to information exchanged and conduct undertaken in connection with this Annex B prior to termination, in accordance with their terms.

### B.14 Termination

B.14.1 In addition to the termination mechanisms in Cl. B.11, B.12, and B.13, this Annex B terminates on the earliest of (a) termination of the Master, (b) written agreement of the Parties, or (c) thirty (30) days' written notice given by either Party to the other expressly terminating this Annex B.

B.14.2 Termination of this Annex B (other than under Cl. B.11.1, B.12, or B.13.1) does not, of itself, terminate the Master or Annex A.

B.14.3 Cl. B.5 (Regulatory Representations), B.9 (Payment), B.10 (Tail), B.11.2 (Clawback), and B.13 (Enhanced Severability) survive termination of this Annex B.
