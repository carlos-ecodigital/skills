## Annex A — Commercial Introductions

This Annex A is part of the Master Introduction Agreement dated [MIA_DATE] between [DE:LEGAL_NAME] (the "**Provider**") and [INTRODUCER_NAME] (the "**Introducer**") (together, the "**MIA**"). Capitalised terms not defined in this Annex A have the meanings given in the Master.

### A.1 Activation and integration

A.1.1 This Annex A is activated by the Parties' execution of this Annex alongside the Master.

A.1.2 The provisions of this Annex A take effect from [ANNEX_A_EFFECTIVE_DATE].

A.1.3 The provisions of the Master apply to and form part of this Annex A. In the event of conflict between this Annex A and the Master, the Master prevails, except in respect of matters expressly addressed by this Annex A (in which case this Annex A prevails).

### A.2 Definitions specific to Annex A

"**Capacity Bucket**" means one of the three capacity bands set out in Cl. A.4.3 below.

"**Customer**" means a third party introduced by the Introducer to the Provider with a view to that party becoming a paying customer of the Provider's colocation services under an MSA.

"**MRR**" means the monthly recurring revenue payable by a Customer to the Provider under an MSA, calculated by reference to the colocation capacity contracted (in EUR per kW per month) and the duration of the contracted term, exclusive of pass-through energy charges, exclusive of one-off fees, and exclusive of VAT.

"**MSA**" has the meaning given in the Master.

"**Qualifying Commercial Introduction**" has the meaning given in Cl. A.3.

### A.3 Qualifying Commercial Introduction

A.3.1 A "**Qualifying Commercial Introduction**" arises when all of the following have occurred in respect of the same Customer:
(a) the Introducer has made a written introduction of the Customer to the Provider;
(b) the Provider has accepted the introduction in writing;
(c) the Customer has executed an MSA with the Provider; and
(d) the Customer has paid the first invoice issued under that MSA in cleared funds.

A.3.2 The Pipeline Carve-Out in Clause 7 of the Master applies to all Introductions under this Annex A. No Fee is due in respect of a Customer that falls within Clause 7.1 of the Master.

### A.4 Fee schedule

A.4.1 **Election.** For each Qualifying Commercial Introduction, the Introducer elects, at the time of the original written introduction (Cl. A.3.1(a)), one of the two fee bases set out below (Cl. A.4.3 — Tiered Residual or Cl. A.4.4 — Capitalised One-Time). The election is binding for the life of that Introduction.

A.4.2 **Capacity Bucket.** The Capacity Bucket applicable to a Qualifying Commercial Introduction is determined by reference to the contracted colocation capacity (in MW IT) under the executed MSA, as set out in Cl. A.4.3.

A.4.3 **Option 1 — Tiered Residual % MRR.**

| Capacity Bucket | Capacity contracted (MW IT) | Residual fee (% of MRR) |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Bucket 1 | < 5 MW IT | 8–10% of MRR |
| Bucket 2 | 5–20 MW IT | 6–8% of MRR |
| Bucket 3 | > 20 MW IT | 4–6% of MRR |

The exact percentage within each band is set in Schedule A-1 at the time of the Qualifying Commercial Introduction. The Fee is payable monthly in arrears on the Provider's receipt of MRR from the Customer, for the initial term of the MSA. Renewal terms are negotiated separately.

A.4.4 **Option 2 — Capitalised One-Time (months MRR equivalent).**

| Capacity Bucket | Capacity contracted (MW IT) | Capitalised fee |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Bucket 1 | < 5 MW IT | 3 months MRR equivalent |
| Bucket 2 | 5–20 MW IT | 2 months MRR equivalent |
| Bucket 3 | > 20 MW IT | 1.5 months MRR equivalent |

The Fee is payable in a single lump sum within thirty (30) days of the Customer's payment of the first invoice under the MSA (Cl. A.3.1(d)).

A.4.5 **Default presentation order.** Where the Introducer does not make an election at the time of the original written introduction, the Capitalised One-Time basis (Option 2) applies by default.

### A.5 Payment

A.5.1 **Per-execution detail.** Currency, invoicing entity, payable-to entity, payment terms, and VAT/withholding treatment for each Qualifying Commercial Introduction are set out in Schedule A-1. Default position: EUR, Net 30, VAT treatment per the recipient jurisdiction's reverse-charge rules where applicable.

A.5.2 **Set-off.** The Provider may set off any amount owed by the Introducer to the Provider under this MIA against any Fee due under this Annex A.

A.5.3 **Disputed amounts.** If the Introducer disputes any Fee calculation in good faith, the undisputed portion is payable in accordance with Cl. A.5.1, and the Parties shall use reasonable endeavours to resolve the dispute within thirty (30) days.

### A.6 Tail

A.6.1 The Introducer's right to receive a Fee in respect of a Customer (and the Provider's obligation to pay it) survives termination of this Annex A (and of the Master) for any Qualifying Commercial Introduction where conditions Cl. A.3.1(a), (b), and (c) are satisfied within the period ending [ANNEX_A_TAIL] after termination.

A.6.2 No Fee accrues under this Annex A in respect of an Introduction made for the first time more than [ANNEX_A_TAIL] after termination.

### A.7 Termination

A.7.1 This Annex A terminates on the earliest of (a) termination of the Master, (b) written agreement of the Parties, or (c) thirty (30) days' written notice given by either Party to the other expressly terminating this Annex A.

A.7.2 Termination of this Annex A does not, of itself, terminate the Master or Annex B.

A.7.3 Cl. A.5 (Payment) and Cl. A.6 (Tail) survive termination of this Annex A.
