# Internal Glossary — DE acronyms

Internal glossary for Digital Energy-specific acronyms. Consumed by AI operators across `legal-assistant`, `de-executive-comms`, and sibling skills. When writing customer-facing content, prefer full expansions on first mention.

## Core product

| Acronym | Expansion | Scope |
|---|---|---|
| **DEC** | Digital Energy Center | Core product — distributed energy hubs for liquid-cooled AI colocation |
| **DEC Block** | 1.2 MW IT standardised compute unit | Internal-only; banned in customer clauses (R-4) |
| **NCP** | NeoCloud Platform | Used in cover emails, LOI tails |

## Usage note

When a counterparty is outside DE's immediate ecosystem (first-time LOI), expand on first mention:

> "…under a Master Services Agreement (MSA) to follow…"

Internal-facing content (WBR, ops meetings, domain cards) may use acronyms directly without expansion.

Banned cross-reference (R-4): **"DEC Block"** is an internal manufacturing/programme term. Never surface it in customer clauses — use "data centre module" or "1.2 MW IT compute unit" in LOI body text if an equivalent is needed.
