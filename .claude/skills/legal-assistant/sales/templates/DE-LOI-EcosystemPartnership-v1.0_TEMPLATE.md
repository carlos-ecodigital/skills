# DE-LOI-EP-v1.0 — Ecosystem Partnership LOI (Reference Template)

**Status:** v1.0 reference template, 2026-04-16. Generator engine produces cover + Recital A + Recital B automatically; clauses 1–7 below are drafted from this markdown.

**Use for:** Relationships with no direct commercial flow — standards bodies, universities, research consortia, co-marketing alliances, industry associations, policy partners. Co-positioning and collaboration framework only.

**Do NOT use for:**
- Any relationship with commercial flow (use EU / DS / WS / SS)
- Customer capacity purchase (use EU or WS)
- Distribution / channel (use DS)
- Equipment / service supply (use SS)
- Investor / capital introductions (use DE-MIA Annex B via `legal-counsel`)

**If in doubt:** If commercial flow is contemplated within 12 months, use a commercial-type LOI. EP is strictly non-commercial.

## 1. Definitions (minimal)

**1.1** In this LOI, unless the context requires otherwise:

- *"Confidential Information"* means all information (whether technical, commercial, financial, or otherwise) disclosed by a Party to the other in connection with the Collaboration, whether in writing, orally, electronically, or by inspection, including any information that by its nature or the circumstances of disclosure would reasonably be understood to be confidential. The existence and contents of this LOI are Confidential Information.
- *"Collaboration"* means the ecosystem activities and joint work contemplated by this LOI as described in Clause 3.
- *"Representatives"* means, in relation to a Party, its Affiliates and its and their respective directors, officers, employees, agents, and professional advisers.

No `DEC`, `Services`, `MSA`, `Sales Order Form`, `Competitor`, `Protected Business Information` — commercial scaffolding not applicable.

---

## 2. Purpose and Scope

**2.1** This LOI records the Parties' mutual interest in a non-commercial ecosystem collaboration around [COLLABORATION_THEMES].

**2.2** The collaboration is strictly non-commercial. No Party shall be entitled to any fee, payment, capacity, discount, or commercial benefit from the other Party by reason of this LOI or any activity under it.

**2.3** The commercial implications of any future activity — including any joint offering, research licensing, commercialisation of joint output, or provision of services by either Party to the other — shall be the subject of a separate agreement and are expressly outside the scope of this LOI.

**2.4** The confidentiality and general provisions in Clauses 6 and 7 are legally binding and enforceable from the date of execution. Clauses 3 through 5 are non-binding expressions of collaborative intent.

## 5. Intellectual Property and Deliverables

**5.1 Background IP.** Each Party retains all right, title, and interest in its background intellectual property. Nothing in this LOI transfers or licenses any intellectual property between the Parties except as expressly set out in Clause 4.2.

**5.2 Joint Deliverables.** Any intellectual property jointly developed by the Parties in the course of the collaboration (including joint publications, working-group outputs, and jointly authored materials) shall be governed by a separate agreement to be executed before the joint activity produces any material output.

**5.3 No Assignment by Participation.** Nothing in this LOI or in any activity conducted under it shall be construed as an assignment, licence, or transfer of intellectual property.

**5.4 Publication.** Each Party retains the right to publish, present, and disseminate its own work, subject to the confidentiality provisions in Clause 6.

---

## 6. Confidentiality (BINDING, Tier A — mutual, light)

**6.1** Each Party shall keep confidential all Confidential Information received from the other Party and shall use such information solely for the Collaboration.

**6.2** Each Party may disclose Confidential Information only to its Representatives who have a genuine need to know for the Collaboration and who are bound by confidentiality obligations no less restrictive than this Clause 6.

**6.3 Exclusions.** The obligations in Clauses 6.1 and 6.2 do not apply to information that the receiving Party can demonstrate: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the receiving Party; (b) was already in the receiving Party's possession without restriction; (c) was independently developed without use of the Confidential Information; or (d) was received from a third party without breach of any confidentiality obligation.

**6.4 Compelled Disclosure.** A Party may disclose Confidential Information to the extent required by applicable law, regulation, or court order, provided that (where legally permitted) the disclosing Party gives the other Party prior written notice and discloses only the minimum required.

**6.5 Return and Destruction.** Upon written request or expiry of this LOI, the receiving Party shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information and certify such return or destruction in writing. Copies required by applicable law or internal compliance policies may be retained, subject to continued confidentiality.

**6.6 Announcements.** Public statements about this LOI are governed by Clause 4.

**6.7 Survival.** The obligations in this Clause 6 shall survive for 2 years from the date of termination or expiry of this LOI.

---

## 7. General Provisions (BINDING)

**7.1 Non-Binding Status.**
(a) Non-binding provisions. Clauses 2, 3, 4, and 5 of this LOI are non-binding expressions of collaborative intent.
(b) Binding provisions. Clauses 6 (Confidentiality) and 7 (General Provisions) are legally binding and enforceable obligations.

**7.2 No Commercial Commitment.** Nothing in this LOI creates any obligation on either Party to pay, purchase, supply, or grant anything of commercial value to the other. Any such arrangement requires a separate agreement.

**7.3 Non-Exclusivity.** As per Clause 3.5.

**7.4 Term and Validity.** This LOI shall remain valid for a period of 12 months from the date of execution, renewable by mutual written agreement. Upon lapse, Clause 6 (Confidentiality) shall survive for its stated period.

**7.5 Costs.** Each Party shall bear its own costs in connection with this LOI and the Collaboration.

**7.6 Counterparts.** This LOI may be executed in counterparts, including by electronic signature within the meaning of the eIDAS Regulation (EU) No 910/2014.

**7.7 Notices.** All notices under this LOI shall be in writing (including email) and addressed to the contact details in the preamble. A notice is effective upon receipt.

**7.8 Governing Law.** This LOI shall be governed by the laws of the Netherlands. The United Nations Convention on Contracts for the International Sale of Goods (CISG) is expressly excluded.

**7.9 Jurisdiction.** The courts of Amsterdam (Rechtbank Amsterdam) shall have exclusive jurisdiction over any dispute arising out of or in connection with the binding provisions of this LOI.

**7.10 Entire Agreement.** This LOI constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties in relation to its subject matter and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and agreements. Nothing in this Clause limits liability for fraud.

**7.11 Partnership Disclaimer.** Nothing in this LOI shall be construed as creating a partnership, joint venture, agency, or employment relationship between the Parties. Neither Party has authority to bind the other or to incur any obligation on the other's behalf.
