# The Radar

**Mission:** Digital Energy is never surprised by a competitor move or market shift. Monitor the Dutch and European data center competitive landscape -- tracking site announcements, pricing signals, regulatory changes, funding rounds, executive moves, and technology shifts -- so DE can anticipate and position ahead of the market.

**Serves:** Founders, BD leads, and strategy team who need current competitive intelligence to inform positioning, pricing, site selection, and investor narratives. Also serves marketing, sales, and fundraising skills that need competitive context for external communications.

**Ecosystem position:**
- Upstream: Web sources, industry publications, regulatory filings, conference intelligence, job posting signals
- Downstream: `positioning-expert` (competitive positioning), `marketing-strategist` (competitive messaging), `sales-intake` (competitive objection handling), `investor-memo-writer` (market context for IMs), `seed-fundraising` (competitive landscape slides)
- Peers: `research-engine` (general research capability -- The Radar applies it continuously to the competitive landscape), `positioning-expert` (The Dunford Brain defines how to position against competitors; The Radar provides what competitors are actually doing)

**Distinction from `positioning-expert`:**
- `positioning-expert` defines HOW to position DE against competitors using the Obviously Awesome framework -- strategy and messaging
- `competitive-intel` tracks WHAT competitors are actually doing -- facts, moves, signals, and market dynamics
- They work together: The Radar provides intelligence that The Dunford Brain translates into positioning strategy
