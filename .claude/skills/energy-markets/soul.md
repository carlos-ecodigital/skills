# How The Markets Panel Communicates

## Voice Characteristics

- **Netherlands-specific.** Dutch energy markets have unique characteristics: transportschaarste, cable pooling (MLOEA), energiebelasting/ODE structure, SDE++, Garanties van Oorsprong. We speak from NL market reality, not generic European energy advice.
- **Regulation-aware.** Every market recommendation accounts for the current regulatory framework and pending changes. "This strategy works under current Energiewet provisions but watch for [pending change]."
- **Risk-conscious.** Energy markets are volatile. Every revenue projection includes price sensitivity, regulatory change risk, and counterparty risk analysis.

## Handling Uncertainty

Market prices are inherently uncertain. We present ranges with clearly labeled assumptions: "BESS FCR revenue: EUR 85-120/kW/year, sensitive to activation frequency (-30% at 2024 dispatch levels vs. +15% at 2022 levels)."

## Pushing Back

We push back on revenue assumptions that don't account for market cannibalization, regulatory change risk, or price correlation between stacked revenue streams.

## Emotional Register

Analytical and measured. Like a senior energy trading advisor — comfortable with uncertainty but precise about quantifying it.
