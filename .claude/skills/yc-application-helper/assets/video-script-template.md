# YC Founder Video Script — [Company Name]

**Format spec (per ARC-029, Andersen):**
- 60 seconds
- All founders on camera
- No music, no fancy effects
- Founders speak directly to camera; no narration over visuals
- YouTube-hostable

**Content sequence:**
1. Founder names + what the company does (HALE-002 — lead with what)
2. Why now / specific traction proof point with named number
3. Why us (1 line per founder — the formidability anchor from P-ACC-2)
4. Close: ask (joining YC for [specific reason])

**Spoken word budget:** ~150 words for 60 seconds. Faster pace possible but reduces clarity.

## Anti-pattern checks

Per ANTI-011 (product-focused video):
- [ ] Founders are on camera the whole time (not just product screens)
- [ ] No narration over product visuals replacing founder presence
- [ ] Each founder speaks at least once

Per ARC-029 format spec:
- [ ] No background music
- [ ] No fancy effects, transitions, animations
- [ ] Total length ≤ 60 seconds
- [ ] Hosted on YouTube (or hostable)

Per HALE-002 / SEIBEL-005 voice:
- [ ] Lead with what, not why or how
- [ ] Sincere / earnest tone — not rehearsed-pitch register
- [ ] Founder voice preserved in v-polished (substance preserved, only register polished)

---

## Production checklist

- [ ] Filming location decided (avoid cluttered backgrounds; natural light if possible)
- [ ] Each founder rehearsed their lines (not memorized — internalized)
- [ ] One take recorded to verify pacing
- [ ] All founders visible on camera throughout (or clear handoffs)
- [ ] Script timed at ≤60 seconds when read at conversational pace
- [ ] Uploaded to YouTube (unlisted is fine for application)
