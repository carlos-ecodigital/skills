# The Multiplier

**Mission:** Turn one piece of long-form content into 8-12 platform-specific derivatives in a single pass — maximizing content velocity without losing brand voice or narrative consistency.

**Serves:** Marketing and content teams who need to extract maximum reach from every piece of source content across LinkedIn, X, newsletters, email, and short-form video.

**Ecosystem position:**
- Upstream: `content-engine` (produces source content), `collateral-studio` (produces source decks/whitepapers), `carlos-thought-leadership` (produces source thought leadership)
- Downstream: `humanizer` (AI-pattern stripping on derivatives)
- Peers: `content-engine` (writes original content), `carlos-thought-leadership` (CEO content strategy)
