# Digital Energy -- Brand Identity

## Mission

Build Europe's sovereign AI infrastructure by co-locating Digital Energy Centers on industrial and agricultural sites, recycling 100% of compute energy into usable heat, and turning grid scarcity into structural competitive advantage.

## Vision

Every megawatt of AI compute in Europe generates a megawatt of useful heat. Digital Energy makes this the default, not the exception.

## Values

| Value | Meaning | How It Shows in Communications |
|---|---|---|
| **Precision** | We deal in verifiable facts, not aspirational claims | Every number cited, every regulatory reference current, every financial claim sourced |
| **Speed** | Infrastructure development measured in months, not years | Concrete timelines: Fonti 4 months, BESS 12 months, Powergrow 12 months from FID |
| **Integration** | We don't sell one product — we build ecosystems | Always show the full stack: compute + heat + BESS + grid + finance |
| **Honesty** | Sophisticated counterparties respect intellectual honesty | Present three scenarios (conservative/base/optimistic); acknowledge unknowns |
| **Partnership** | We build with, not for | Consortium model: Lenovo, Nokia, Ampower, grower networks, institutional investors |

## Messaging Pillars

Five operating principles that define what DE does and why it works. These are industrial principles, not marketing slogans.

| # | Pillar | Core Principle | Lead Audiences |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Multi-use infrastructure** | Every resource serves multiple purposes simultaneously. One grid connection → compute + BESS + heat. Digital cogeneration (CHC): compute + heat from one energy input. | Growers, investors |
| 2 | **Maximum utilization of underutilized assets** | Find stranded resources. Activate them. Oversized grower grid connections, retiring coal plant infrastructure, agricultural land with zoning flexibility. | Investors, growers, policy |
| 3 | **Sovereign European AI** | European compute for European data, built on European soil, by European partners. Sovereignty by architecture, not by contract clause. | Neoclouds, enterprises, policy |
| 4 | **Flexible IT loads on an open grid** | DECs are active grid participants, not passive consumers. BESS + demand response + workload flexibility = grid stabilization. "Energy maker, not taker." | Policy, municipalities, investors |
| 5 | **Decentralized energy infrastructure for distributed intelligence** | Compute should be everywhere — fast, local, economical. 4.2 MW modular blocks, deployable anywhere there's grid + thermal demand. The Super-Factory. | Neoclouds, enterprises, strategic |

**Pillar hierarchy (when space is limited):**

| Context | Lead With | Support With |
|---|---|---|
| Grower conversation | Pillar 1 + 2 | Digital cogeneration frame |
| Neocloud pitch | Pillar 5 + 3 | Speed, latency, grid access |
| Investor presentation | Pillar 2 + 1 | All pillars map to returns |
| Policy / municipality | Pillar 4 + 2 | Grid stabilization, permitting by design |
| Enterprise | Pillar 3 + 5 | Compliance, performance |
| Press / thought leadership | Pillar 5 + 1 | Vision narrative |
