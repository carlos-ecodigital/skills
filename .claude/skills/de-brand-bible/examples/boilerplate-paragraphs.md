# Digital Energy -- Boilerplate Paragraphs

Standard "about" text in multiple lengths and languages for use across marketing materials.

## English — Long (100 words)

Digital Energy Group AG develops sovereign AI infrastructure across Europe by co-locating Digital Energy Centers (DECs) on industrial and agricultural sites. Each DEC recycles 100% of compute thermal energy into usable heat for greenhouses, district heating networks, and industrial users — eliminating cooling costs while generating thermal production revenue. The company activates existing grid connections in capacity-constrained markets, deploys battery energy storage for grid services, and structures each project as a non-recourse financed Dutch BV. Digital Energy's vertically integrated digital cogeneration model combines compute, energy recycling, BESS, and project finance into a single development platform. Headquartered in Zug, Switzerland, with operations in the Netherlands.
