# Digital Energy Meeting System — Standard Operating Procedure

**Version:** 2.0
**Effective:** 2026-04-14 (Week 2 — 2-meeting split live)
**Owner:** Carlos Reuven (CEO) + Yoni Fishman (Engine Operator)

---

## 1. Purpose

This SOP defines how Digital Energy runs internal meetings. It replaces the previous ad-hoc all-hands format with a structured 2-meeting weekly system, plus format standards for all recurring meetings.

The system is designed to:
- Maximize decisions per minute of meeting time
- Eliminate duplicate status reporting
- Create visible accountability without CEO nagging
- Generate team energy and shared mission identity
- Free CEO time from meetings that don't require CEO presence

## 3. Pre-Read System

### What
Every department head submits a 1-page written update by Friday 17:00 CET using the standard template (`templates/pre-read-template.md`).

### Why
Written updates replace verbal status reporting. Meeting time is for decisions, not broadcasts. This also replaces the previous weekly report system — the pre-read IS the weekly report.

### Template Structure
1. Last week's commitments: GREEN (delivered) or RED (missed + reason)
2. This week's top 3 deliverables (noun + verb + deadline)
3. Blockers requiring cross-functional help
4. One number that matters
5. Decision needed from Leadership Decisions? (Y/N + details)

### Enforcement
| Time | Action |
|------|--------|
| Thu 17:00 | Auto-reminder sent |
| Fri 12:00 | Warning to non-submitters: "Will be listed as MISSING" |
| Fri 17:00 | Deadline. Missing = "No update submitted by [name]" |
| Sat | AI generates CEO Brief. Missing departments = RED in scorecard |

## 4. DE Weekly (Monday 17:00, 30 min, Full Team)

### Owner: Carlos (CEO)
### Facilitator: Yoni (Engine Operator)

### Format
- [0:00-0:05] CEO Opening: mission anchor, win of the week, risk of the week, ONE priority
- [0:05-0:15] Accountability flash: company score, RED items (no discussion)
- [0:15-0:25] Spotlight or Q&A
- [0:25-0:30] CEO Close: repeat 3 priorities + owners + deadlines, forward energy

### What This Meeting Is NOT
- Not a status update round (those are in writing)
- Not a technical discussion forum (those go to dedicated sessions)
- Not a democracy of equal speaking time (this is a CEO-led broadcast)
- Not optional (full team attendance expected, recording available for absentees)

---

## 5. Leadership Decisions (Monday 17:45, 30 min, Core Team)

### Owner: Carlos (CEO)
### Facilitator: Yoni (Engine Operator)
### Attendees: Carlos, Yoni, Jelmer, Jonathan, Santiago, Halyna/Lucie

### Format
- [0:00-0:02] Frame: N decisions in 28 minutes
- [0:02-0:27] Decision blocks: 5 min each, context → options → discussion → decision
- [0:27-0:30] Close: read back decisions, disagree-and-commit

### Rules
- No presentations. Everyone read the pre-reads.
- No status updates. Decisions only.
- 5-minute max per item. Complex items get separate sessions.
- Disagree and commit. Once decided, everyone rows together.

## 6. Accountability Scorecard

### What
A cumulative week-over-week tracker that marks every commitment as GREEN (delivered) or RED (missed).

### Metrics
- Company-level GREEN rate (target: 80%+)
- Department-level scores
- RED items with explanations + new deadlines
- Rolled-over items (2+ weeks = CEO escalation)
- Pre-read compliance rate

### Visibility
Shared with full team as part of DE Weekly. No hiding, no softening.

## 10. Meeting Hygiene Rules

1. Join 1 minute early. Meeting starts on time.
2. Camera ON (<20 people, non-negotiable).
3. Mute when not speaking.
4. No "can you hear me" loops — fix offline, rejoin.
5. No echo/dual-device setups.
6. No WhatsApp for meeting content — ClickUp or email only.
7. Substance over clock — judge meetings by decisions/outputs produced, not minutes elapsed. Remote teams need real time together.
8. If you're not adding value, leave. It's not rude.
