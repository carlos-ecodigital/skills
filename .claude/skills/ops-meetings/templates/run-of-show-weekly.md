# DE Weekly — Run of Show
**Monday 09:00 CET | 30 minutes | Full Team**

---

## [0:00 - 0:05] CEO OPENING (Carlos)

- **Mission anchor** (1 sentence — same every week, reinforced)
- **Win of the week** — name the person, name the output, frame as mission-evidence
- **Risk of the week** — what's at stake, why it matters, no sugarcoating
- **The ONE priority** — "If you do nothing else this week: [X]"

---

## [0:05 - 0:15] ACCOUNTABILITY FLASH (Yoni reads, Carlos comments)

- Company-level GREEN rate: [X]%
- RED items by department (1 line each — no discussion, just visibility)
- Rolled-over items (2+ weeks — flag only, discussion in Leadership Sync)

*Purpose: Everyone sees who delivered and who didn't. No lectures. The numbers speak.*

---

## [0:15 - 0:25] SPOTLIGHT + Q&A

Option A: **Team Spotlight** (rotating weekly)
- One team member gets 3 minutes to share something they're working on, a lesson learned, or a tool/technique that's working
- Builds shared identity and cross-functional awareness

Option B: **Open Q&A**
- "What questions do you have about the pre-reads or this week's priorities?"
- Max 3 questions, 2 min each
- Complex questions → "Let's take that to Leadership Sync"

---

## [0:25 - 0:30] CEO CLOSE (Carlos)

- **Repeat the 3 priorities** for the week — name owners, name deadlines
- **Forward look** — "Here's what we're building toward. Here's why it matters."
- **Close** — energy, not logistics. "Let's have a great week" is banned. Replace with: "Let's go win [specific thing]."

---

## Rules

- Camera ON for all attendees
- Mute when not speaking
- No status updates from the floor — those are in the pre-read
- Technical questions → "Good question, let's discuss in [relevant meeting]"
- Meeting ends at 0:30 regardless. No overruns.
- If Carlos is unavailable: Yoni runs a shortened 15-min version (scorecard + priorities only)

---

## Post-Meeting (Yoni, by 10:30)

- Process Fireflies transcript
- Send 1-page summary to full company
- Route action items to ClickUp
- Seed next week's pre-read with carry-forward items
