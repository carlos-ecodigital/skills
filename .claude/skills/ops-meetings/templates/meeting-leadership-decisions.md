# Leadership Decisions — Run of Show
**Monday 17:45 CET | 30 minutes | Core Team Only**

**Attendees:** Carlos, Yoni, Jelmer, Jonathan, Santiago, Halyna/Lucie (5-7 max)
**Others:** Invited for specific items only. Join for their item, leave after.

---

## [0:00 - 0:02] FRAME (Carlos)

- "We have [N] decisions to make in 28 minutes. Let's go."
- Read the decision agenda (pre-distributed in CEO Brief)

---

## [0:02 - 0:27] DECISION BLOCKS

**Item 1: [Topic]** — Owner: [name] — 5 min
```
[0:00] Context: Owner states the decision in 1 sentence
[0:01] Options: A, B, or C — 1 sentence each
[0:02] Discussion: Input from relevant people
[0:04] Decision: Carlos decides or delegates. Group commits.
[0:05] Record: Yoni logs: decision, owner, deadline
```

**Item 2: [Topic]** — Owner: [name] — 5 min
*(Same format)*

**Item 3: [Topic]** — Owner: [name] — 5 min
*(Same format)*

**Item 4: [Topic]** — Owner: [name] — 5 min
*(Same format)*

**Blocker Escalations** — 5 min
- Items surfaced from pre-reads that aren't decisions but need CEO attention
- Carlos: resolve, delegate, or schedule a separate session

---

## [0:27 - 0:30] CLOSE (Carlos)

- Read back all decisions made (Yoni confirms)
- "Any decision I missed or you disagree with? Speak now."
- If no objection: "Committed. Let's execute."

---

## Rules

- **No presentations.** Everyone read the pre-reads. If you didn't, you can't participate.
- **No status updates.** This is a decision meeting, not an update meeting.
- **Disagree and commit.** Once a decision is made, everyone rows the same direction. Relitigating is banned until the next review cycle.
- **5-minute max per item.** If it needs more time, it gets a dedicated 30-min session with only relevant people.
- **If an item has no clear options, it's not ready for this meeting.** Send it back to the department head to prepare properly.

---

## Post-Meeting (Yoni)

- Log all decisions in decision-tracker format
- Route action items to ClickUp with owners + deadlines
- Include decisions in post-meeting summary email
