---

## Rules

- **No thermodynamics debates.** Technical deep-dives go to separate sessions.
- **Bring options, not problems.** If you have a blocker, bring 2 solutions.
- **Vendor follow-ups are async.** Don't spend meeting time composing emails.
- **If it's not on the agenda, it waits.** Add it to next week's decision queue.
- **Every segment must produce an output.** If a 2-hour session resolves 8 vendor decisions, that's a great meeting. If a 30-minute session resolves zero, that's a bad one. Judge by substance, not clock.
