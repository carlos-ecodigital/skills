**Subject:** How we're running meetings starting this week

---

Team,

We've had two all-hands meetings so far. They were a good start — getting everyone in the same room was the right call. But I want to be honest about what's not working, and what we're changing.

**What's not working:**

Our meetings are status update rounds. Everyone reports what they're doing, but we don't review what we committed to doing last week. We don't make decisions together. And most of you sit through 40 minutes of updates that don't affect your work.

We also have overlap — the same topics come up in procurement, GTM, and the all-hands. That's 17 people listening to things 6 people already discussed.

At the stage we're at — pre-revenue, fundraising, 14 projects to move — every hour in a meeting that doesn't produce a decision is an hour we don't get back.

**What's changing:**

Starting this week, we're introducing **written pre-reads**. Every department head will submit a 1-page update by Friday at 17:00. The template is simple:

1. What you committed to last week — delivered or not (GREEN/RED)
2. Your top 3 deliverables this week (specific output + deadline)
3. Blockers that need help from other teams
4. One number that matters

This replaces the verbal status updates in the meeting. Everyone reads before Monday. Meeting time is for decisions and alignment only.

**Starting next week (April 14)**, the meeting itself will split into two:

- **DE Weekly** (Monday 09:00, 30 min, everyone) — I'll open with the company's biggest win, biggest risk, and the one thing that matters most this week. Then Q&A. Then close.
- **Leadership Sync** (Monday 09:45, 30 min, department heads only) — Decisions and blockers. No presentations. Just choices.

**Why this matters:**

We're building something unprecedented in European infrastructure. The quality of our execution has to match the ambition of what we're building. That starts with how we run our meetings — they need to be the sharpest, most honest, most decisive 30 minutes of the week.

Yoni will be sending out the pre-read template this Thursday. Please have your first one submitted by Friday.

Let's go build.

Carlos
