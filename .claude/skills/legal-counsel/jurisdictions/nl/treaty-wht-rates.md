## EU Parent-Subsidiary Directive

- **0% WHT** on dividends paid by NL subsidiary to qualifying EU/EEA parent with 5%+ holding
- Requires: genuine economic activity, not purely artificial arrangement
- Anti-abuse provisions apply (Art. 4 Wet DB)

## EU Interest and Royalties Directive

- **0%** on interest and royalties between associated companies in EU member states
- Associated = 25%+ direct holding
- Subject to beneficial ownership and anti-abuse requirements

## Inhoudingsvrijstelling (WHT Exemption, Art. 4 lid 2 Wet DB)

- 0% WHT for qualifying holding BVs/Cooperaties meeting substance requirements
- Available for both treaty and non-treaty jurisdictions (within EU/EEA, or where treaty applies)
- Requires: substance in NL, no artificial arrangement, beneficial ownership

## Conditional Withholding Tax (Wet bronbelasting 2021)

- **25.8%** on interest, royalties, and certain other payments to:
  - Low-tax jurisdictions (effective rate <9%)
  - EU blacklisted jurisdictions
  - Abusive structures
- Applies since 2021; supplements treaty network
- Specific exemptions for genuine business reasons

## Key Considerations

- **MLI (Multilateral Instrument):** modifies many Dutch treaties; PPT (principal purposes test) added to most
- **LOB (limitation on benefits):** present in NL-US treaty; complex eligibility tests
- **Beneficial ownership:** required for treaty benefits; substance-over-form analysis
- **Anti-abuse provisions:** Art. 4 Wet DB and general anti-abuse principle
- Always verify current treaty text and MLI positions at belastingdienst.nl
