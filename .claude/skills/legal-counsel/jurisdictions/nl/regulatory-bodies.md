# Netherlands -- Regulatory Bodies

## Financial and Securities

| Authority | Role | Relevance |
|---|---|---|
| **AFM** (Autoriteit Financiele Markten) | Securities supervision, market conduct, prospectus approval | Fund structuring, bond issuance, AIFMD registration/license |
| **DNB** (De Nederlandsche Bank) | Prudential supervision, banking license, insurance supervision | Banking license assessment for SPV lending; depositary oversight; DORA |
| **Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens** (AP) | Data protection (GDPR/AVG enforcement) | DC operators processing personal data; DPIA requirements |

## Competition and Energy

| Authority | Role | Relevance |
|---|---|---|
| **ACM** (Autoriteit Consument en Markt) | Competition, energy regulation, tariff approval | Grid tariffs, cable pooling approval, merger clearance, energy market conduct, heat tariffs (Wcw) |
| **TenneT** | Transmission system operator (TSO) -- hoogspanningsnet (>110 kV) | High-voltage grid connections, congestion management, ancillary services (FCR/aFRR/mFRR) |
| **Liander / Stedin / Enexis** | Regional distribution system operators (DSOs) | Medium/low-voltage connections, transportcapaciteit, cable pooling |

## Enterprise and Subsidies

| Authority | Role | Relevance |
|---|---|---|
| **RVO** (Rijksdienst voor Ondernemend Nederland) | Subsidies, EIA/MIA/WBSO, SDE++ | Subsidy applications, tax deduction certificates, energy project support |
| **Invest-NL** | National development bank | Equity/mezzanine/guarantees for energy transition and digitalisation |
| **PBL** (Planbureau voor de Leefomgeving) | Energy transition analysis, policy advice | Sector analyses, climate policy evaluation |

## Registration and Title

| Authority | Role | Relevance |
|---|---|---|
| **KvK** (Kamer van Koophandel) | Commercial register, UBO register | SPV registration, UBO filings, annual account deposits |
| **Kadaster** (Land Registry) | Registration of real property rights and hypotheken | Hypotheek, opstalrecht, erfpacht registration; title searches |
| **OCNL** (Octrooicentrum Nederland) | Patent registration | Registration of pandrecht on octrooien |

## Tax

| Authority | Role | Relevance |
|---|---|---|
| **Belastingdienst** (Tax and Customs) | Tax collection, VPB/BTW/DB/OB | Tax filings, rulings, registration of stille cessie for date certainty |
| **Douane** | Import/export customs | Relevant for equipment imports (BESS, DC hardware) |

## Permitting and Environment

| Authority | Role | Relevance |
|---|---|---|
| **Gemeente** (Municipality) | Local omgevingsplan, omgevingsvergunning, enforcement | Building permits, OPA, fire safety, participatie |
| **Provinciale Staten / Gedeputeerde Staten** | Provincial policy, omgevingsverordening, nature permits | Natura 2000 vergunning, stikstof, spatial policy, extern salderen |
| **Rijkswaterstaat** | National water and road infrastructure | Watervergunningen near rijkswateren or rijkswegen |
| **ILT** (Inspectie Leefomgeving en Transport) | Environmental enforcement, building quality | BESS safety enforcement, environmental compliance |
| **Veiligheidsregio** | Fire safety, disaster response | BESS fire safety assessment, PGS 37 compliance |
| **Commissie m.e.r.** | Independent EIA advisory body | Mandatory advice on plan-MER; advisory on project-MER |

## Professional Services

| Authority | Role | Relevance |
|---|---|---|
| **Notaris** (civil-law notary) | Authentication of deeds, identity verification | SPV oprichting, share transfers, hypotheek creation, security packages |
| **Advocaat** (lawyer -- orde van advocaten) | Legal representation, legal advice | Mandatory for court proceedings (procesmonopolie in certain courts) |
| **Belastingadviseur** (tax adviser) | Tax advice and compliance | No regulated title in NL but NOB membership indicates quality |
| **Accountant** (auditor -- NBA) | Audit, assurance | Mandatory audit for middelgroot and groot companies |
