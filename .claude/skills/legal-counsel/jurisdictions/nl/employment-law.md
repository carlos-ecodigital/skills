# Netherlands -- Employment Law

## Governing Framework

Dutch employment law is primarily codified in **Boek 7 BW, Titel 10 (Art. 7:610 et seq.)**, supplemented by the Wet werk en zekerheid (WWZ), the Wet arbeidsmarkt in balans (WAB, eff. 1 Jan 2020), and sector-specific CAOs (collective labour agreements).

## Arbeidsovereenkomst (Employment Agreement)

### Essential Elements (Art. 7:610 BW)

- Work performed personally (persoonlijke arbeid)
- In service of the employer (gezagsverhouding / authority relationship)
- Against wages (loon)

### Types of Contracts

| Type | Key Features |
|---|---|
| Onbepaalde tijd (indefinite) | Default; strongest protection for werknemer (employee) |
| Bepaalde tijd (fixed-term) | Max 3 consecutive contracts over max 36 months (ketenregeling, Art. 7:668a BW); 4th contract or exceeding 36 months converts to onbepaalde tijd |
| Oproepovereenkomst (on-call) | After 12 months, employer must offer vast urencontract (fixed hours) based on average hours worked (Art. 7:628a BW) |

### Proeftijd (Probationary Period, Art. 7:652 BW)

- Max 1 month for contracts of 6 months to 2 years
- Max 2 months for contracts > 2 years or indefinite
- Must be in writing; void if not agreed in writing before start

## Ontslag (Dismissal)

### Routes

| Route | Grounds | Procedure |
|---|---|---|
| UWV | Bedrijfseconomische redenen (economic redundancy, a-grond) or langdurige arbeidsongeschiktheid (long-term incapacity >2 years, b-grond) | Application to UWV; ~4-6 weeks |
| Kantonrechter (subdistrict court) | Disfunctioneren (poor performance, d-grond), verwijtbaar handelen (culpable conduct, e-grond), verstoorde arbeidsverhouding (disrupted relationship, g-grond), cumulatiegrond (h-grond), other grounds | Petition to court; ~4-8 weeks |
| Wederzijds goedvinden (mutual consent) | Any | Vaststellingsovereenkomst (settlement agreement); 14-day bedenktijd (cooling-off period, Art. 7:670b BW) |
| Ontslag op staande voet (summary dismissal) | Dringende reden (urgent cause, Art. 7:677 BW) | Immediate; must be given onverwijld (without delay) |

### Opzegtermijn (Notice Period, Art. 7:672 BW)

| Duration of Employment | Employer Notice | Employee Notice |
|---|---|---|
| < 5 years | 1 month | 1 month |
| 5--10 years | 2 months | 1 month |
| 10--15 years | 3 months | 1 month |
| > 15 years | 4 months | 1 month |

CAO or individual agreement may deviate (within limits).

### Transitievergoeding (Transition Payment, Art. 7:673 BW)

- **Calculation:** 1/3 monthly salary per year of service (from day 1)
- **Maximum (2025):** EUR 98,000 (or annual salary if higher)
- Payable upon employer-initiated termination or non-renewal of fixed-term contract
- Not payable if dismissal due to ernstig verwijtbaar handelen (seriously culpable conduct) by employee

## Concurrentiebeding en relatiebeding (Non-Compete and Non-Solicitation)

- **Bepaalde tijd (fixed-term):** non-compete only valid if employer provides schriftelijke motivering (written justification) of zwaarwegende bedrijfsbelangen (compelling business interests, Art. 7:653 lid 2 BW)
- **Onbepaalde tijd (indefinite):** valid if in writing and employee >18 years
- Court may wholly or partially set aside (vernietigen) if employee is onbillijk benadeeld (unfairly prejudiced) in relation to the interest to be protected
- **Duration:** typically 1--2 years; courts increasingly limiting scope and duration

## Wet arbeidsmarkt in balans (WAB, eff. 1 Jan 2020)

Key changes:
- **Cumulatiegrond (h-grond):** allows dismissal based on combination of incomplete dismissal grounds; court may award 50% additional transitievergoeding
- **WW-premie (unemployment insurance):** differentiated premium -- lower for indefinite contracts, higher for fixed-term
- **Payroll workers:** equal terms and conditions as directly employed workers

## Ondernemingsraad (Works Council)

- **Mandatory** for businesses with 50+ employees in NL (Wet op de ondernemingsraden, WOR)
- **Adviesrecht (Art. 25 WOR):** right to advise on major business decisions (restructuring, significant investment, closure, change of management)
- **Instemmingsrecht (Art. 27 WOR):** right to consent on social policy matters (working hours, remuneration systems, privacy regulations)
- Failure to properly consult can result in court annulling the decision

## Arbeidsongeschiktheid (Incapacity for Work)

- Employer must continue paying wages for **104 weeks** (2 years): first 52 weeks 70% (minimum: minimum wage); second 52 weeks 70% (no minimum)
- Many CAOs require 100% in year 1, 70% in year 2
- Employer must follow re-integration obligations (Wet verbetering poortwachter)
- After 2 years: possible dismissal via UWV (b-grond); employee may qualify for WIA benefit

## Key Employment Statutes

| Act | Subject |
|---|---|
| BW Boek 7 Titel 10 | Core employment law |
| WAB | Labour market balance |
| WOR | Works councils |
| WAADI | Temporary agency work |
| WAGA | Cross-border employment (Posted Workers) |
