# Netherlands -- Permitting (Omgevingswet)

## Omgevingswet / Environment and Planning Act (eff. 1 Jan 2024)

Replaces 26 previous laws (including Wro, Wabo, Wet milieubeheer) into a single integrated framework.

## Instruments

| Instrument | Level | Function |
|---|---|---|
| Omgevingsvisie (environmental vision) | Rijk, provincie, gemeente | Strategic long-term vision for the physical environment |
| Omgevingsplan (environment plan) | Gemeente | Replaces bestemmingsplan; all municipal rules in one plan |
| Omgevingsverordening | Provincie | Provincial rules and instructieregels for gemeenten |
| AMvBs (Bal, Bkl, Bbl, Ob) | Rijk | Detailed national rules |
| Omgevingsvergunning | Gemeente (or provincie/Rijk) | Permit for specific activities |

**DSO (Digitaal Stelsel Omgevingswet):** digital system for submitting applications and checking rules; accessible via omgevingsloket.nl.

## Omgevingsvergunning (Environmental Permit)

### Activities Requiring Permit

| Activiteit | Description | Procedure |
|---|---|---|
| Bouwactiviteit (building activity) | Constructing, modifying, demolishing | Regulier (8 weeks) for technical assessment; meldingsplicht for gevolgklasse 1 |
| Omgevingsplanactiviteit (OPA) | Deviating from omgevingsplan rules | Regulier or uitgebreid depending on complexity |
| Milieubelastende activiteit | Environmentally impactful activity | Regulier or uitgebreid; MER may be required |
| Wateractiviteit | Discharge, extraction, works affecting water | Waterschap or Rijkswaterstaat |
| Natuuractiviteit | Impact on Natura 2000, protected species | Provincie; may require passende beoordeling |
| Monumentenactiviteit | Works on protected monuments | Gemeente or Rijksdienst voor Cultureel Erfgoed |

### Procedures

| Procedure | Timeline | Key Features |
|---|---|---|
| Reguliere procedure | 8 weken + 6 weken verlenging | Default; van rechtswege verleend (deemed granted) if not decided in time |
| Uitgebreide procedure (afd. 3.4 Awb) | 26 weken + 6 weken verlenging | Complex activities; mandatory participation; no deemed grant |

**Participatie:** mandatory for all omgevingsvergunning applications; applicant must indicate how stakeholders have been involved (Art. 16.55 lid 6 Omgevingswet).

## Bestemmingsplan Transition

- Existing bestemmingsplannen automatically became part of the transitional omgevingsplan (bruidsschat) on 1 Jan 2024
- Deadline for gemeenten to adopt complete omgevingsplan: **1 January 2032**
- **BOPA (buitenplanse omgevingsplanactiviteit):** permit for activities not conforming to omgevingsplan; replaces old buitenplanse afwijking
- During transition: old rules remain in force as part of omgevingsplan until replaced

## MER (Milieueffectrapportage / Environmental Impact Assessment)

- **Verplichte MER (mandatory EIA):** activities listed in Bijlage V Omgevingsbesluit
- **Beoordelingsplicht (screening):** below-threshold activities -- bevoegd gezag determines if full MER required
- **Commissie m.e.r.:** independent advisory body; mandatory advice for plan-MER; optional for project-MER
- Larger BESS (>50 MW) and large DCs may trigger screening or full MER

## DC and BESS Specific Permitting

### Data Centre Restrictions

| Jurisdiction | Measure | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Amsterdam | Moratorium on all new DC construction | Until at least 2030 |
| National | Hyperscale ban: DCs >70 MW + >100,000 m2 prohibited | Except designated areas (Het Hogeland, Hollands Kroon) |
| Noord-Holland | Restriction on DCs >2,000 m2 or >5 MVA | Provinciale Datacenterstrategie 2025--2027 |
| Haarlemmermeer | Voorbereidingsbesluit limiting new DC development | Effective 2024; protecting Schiphol area grid capacity |

### BESS-Specific

- **Nutsvoorziening ruling:** Rechtbank Midden-Nederland (24 April 2025) ruled BESS qualifies as nutsvoorziening (utility facility) under certain omgevingsplan classifications
- **RVO Handreiking elektriciteitsopslagsystemen:** published July 2024; addresses permitting, safety (PGS 37), spatial planning
- **PGS 37:** safety distances and requirements for lithium-ion battery storage; applies to BESS >50 kWh
- **Brandveiligheid (fire safety):** Bbl requirements; consult Veiligheidsregio for larger installations

## Stikstof (Nitrogen)

- **AERIUS Calculator 2025:** mandatory calculation tool for nitrogen deposition
- **Threshold:** >0.005 mol N/ha/jaar on Natura 2000 triggers vergunningplicht
- **Bouwvrijstelling (construction phase exemption):** abolished by Raad van State 2 November 2022 (ECLI:NL:RVS:2022:3159)
- **Intern salderen (internal offsetting):** no longer permit-free (Raad van State December 2024, ECLI:NL:RVS:2024:5093); now requires natuurvergunning
- **Extern salderen (external offsetting):** possible under provincial beleidsregels; requires 30% afroming (clipping) in most provinces
- **ADC-toets:** alternative assessment if significant effects cannot be excluded (Alternatief, Dwingende redenen, Compensatie)
- **Timeline impact:** nitrogen assessment adds 3--12 months to permitting process

## Wet collectieve warmtevoorziening (Wcw / Collective Heat Act)

- Adopted Eerste Kamer 9 December 2025; effective mid-2026 to 1 January 2027
- Municipalities get regierol (steering role) in heat planning
- Designated heat companies >50% publicly owned (7-year transition for existing operators)
- Cost-based tariff replaces gas-price-linked (warmtewet) tariff
- Existing networks: 14--30 year transition period

## Rechtsbescherming (Legal Protection)

### Bezwaar en beroep (Objection and Appeal)

| Stage | Procedure | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| Bezwaar (objection) | Administrative objection to issuing authority | 6 weeks from decision |
| Beroep (appeal) | Administrative court (bestuursrechter) | 6 weeks from bezwaar decision |
| Hoger beroep (higher appeal) | Afdeling bestuursrechtspraak Raad van State | 6 weeks from beroep decision |
| Voorlopige voorziening (interim measures) | Voorzieningenrechter | During any stage |

- **Relativiteitsvereiste (Art. 8:69a Awb):** appellant can only invoke norms intended to protect their interest
- Third-party appeals against permits can significantly delay projects
