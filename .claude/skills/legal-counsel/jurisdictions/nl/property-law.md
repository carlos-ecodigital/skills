# Netherlands -- Property Law

## Governing Framework

Dutch property law is codified in **Boek 3 BW (Vermogensrecht / Patrimonial Law)** and **Boek 5 BW (Zakelijke rechten / Rights in Rem)**. The numerus clausus principle applies: only rights in rem recognized by law can be created.

## Recht van opstal (Right of Superficies, Art. 5:101--105 BW)

Grants the opstaller the right to own gebouwen, werken, of beplantingen (buildings, works, or plantings) in, on, or above another party's onroerend goed (immovable property). **Critical for BESS, DC, and renewable energy projects** on third-party land.

### Creation and Registration

- Created by **notariele akte** registered at **Kadaster** (Land Registry)
- Registration determines priority and creates goederenrechtelijke werking (effect in rem, opposable to third parties)

### Vestigingsakte (Deed of Establishment) Must Specify

- Duur (duration) and verlengingsvoorwaarden (renewal conditions) -- typical: 25--50 years
- Retributie (ground rent) amount and indexatie (indexation)
- Onderhoud (maintenance) obligations
- Einde en gevolgen (termination and consequences)
- Vergoedingsregeling (compensation arrangement) at end of term
- Hypotheceerbaarheid (mortgageability) of the opstalrecht

### Key Features

- Separates ownership of installation from land ownership
- **Can be hypothecated** (Art. 3:227 BW) -- essential for lender security
- **Prefer opstal over huur (lease)** for installations: creates zakelijk recht (right in rem) vs. persoonlijk recht (personal right); avoids huurbescherming (tenant protection) regime
- Retributieplicht (ground rent obligation) payable to grondeigenaar (landowner)

## Erfpacht (Long Lease, Art. 5:85--100 BW)

Near-eigendom (near-ownership) usage rights over another's onroerend goed.

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Duration | 50--99 years or eeuwigdurend (perpetual) |
| Canon (ground rent) | Periodic payment or afkoopsom (lump sum) |
| Hypothecation | Erfpachtrecht can be hypothecated (Art. 5:98 BW) |
| Use | Gemeenten (municipalities) for ground-lease on industrial sites |

**Erfpachtvoorwaarden (leasehold conditions)** set by grondeigenaar; review for:
- Bestemmingsbeperking (use restriction)
- Toestemmingsvereiste (consent requirement) for transfer or sublease
- Canon herziening (rent review) mechanism
- Sancties on breach (verval erfpacht)

## Pandrecht (Pledge, Art. 3:236 BW)

Security right over roerende zaken (movables) and rechten (rights). Key pledges in project finance:

| Verpand object (Pledged Asset) | Creation Method | Key Points |
|---|---|---|
| Aandelen (shares) in project BV | Notariele akte + registration in aandeelhoudersregister (Art. 2:196 BW) | Lender typically obtains stemrecht (voting rights) on enforcement; openbaar pandrecht preferred for control |
| Bankrekeningen (bank accounts) | Onderhandse akte (private deed) or notariele akte + notification to bank | Include verpandingsverbod waiver in account documentation |
| Vorderingen op afnemers (receivables) | Stille cessie (silent assignment) by private deed; notification converts to openbare cessie on enforcement | Register at Belastingdienst for date certainty (Art. 3:239 BW) |
| Contractuele rechten (contractual rights) | Pandakte referencing specific contracts | Include medewerking (cooperation) clause in underlying contract |
| Verzekeringen (insurance proceeds) | Pandakte + notification to verzekeraar (insurer) | Bankersclausule standard in NL insurance market |
| Roerende installaties (movable equipment) | Bezitloos pandrecht (non-possessory pledge) by private deed | Register at Belastingdienst for date certainty; bodemrecht risk |
| Intellectuele eigendom (IP) | Pandakte; for octrooien (patents): registration at OCNL | Relevant for proprietary software |
| Huurvorderingen (rental claims) | Stille cessie by private deed | If project generates rental income |

### Bodemrecht (Tax Authority Priority)

The Belastingdienst has a preferential right (bodemrecht) over movable assets on the debtor's premises (Art. 22 Invorderingswet). This can affect non-possessory pledges on equipment. Mitigation: bodemverhuurconstructie (floor lease construction) -- but its effectiveness is limited after 2013 amendments.

## Hypotheek (Mortgage, Art. 3:260 BW)

Security over onroerend goed and zakelijke rechten (opstal, erfpacht).

- **Must be created by notariele akte** registered at **Kadaster**
- **Recht van parate executie (summary foreclosure, Art. 3:268 BW):** mortgagee may sell without court intervention upon default. Standard in project finance.
- **Priority:** determined by date and time of registration at Kadaster (Art. 3:21 BW)
- **Hypotheekakte** must specify: maximum secured amount, interest reference, enforcement conditions
- Multiple hypotheken possible on same asset; priority by registration date
- **Huurbeding (rental clause, Art. 3:264 BW):** mortgagee may stipulate no lease without consent

## Cessie (Assignment, Art. 3:94 BW)

| Type | Requirements | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Openbare cessie (disclosed) | Akte van cessie + mededeling aan debiteur (notification to debtor) | Enforcement of silent pledges; outright transfer of receivables |
| Stille cessie (silent, Art. 3:94 lid 3 BW) | Onderhandse akte without notification; register at Belastingdienst for date certainty | Security assignment in project finance; convert to openbaar on enforcement |

## Eigendomsvoorbehoud (Retention of Title, Art. 3:92 BW)

- Seller retains ownership until full payment
- Effective against buyer's creditors in insolvency
- Must be agreed in writing before or at delivery
- **Extended retention of title** (for processed goods or further sale) limited under Dutch law; less broad than German Verlangerte Eigentumsvorbehalt

## Kadaster Registration System

- All registergoederen (registrable assets): onroerend goed, schepen (ships), luchtvaartuigen (aircraft)
- Registration provides goederenrechtelijke werking and priority
- Electronic system: kadaster.nl for title searches, registration of deeds
- **Notaris** responsible for preparing and filing deeds at Kadaster
