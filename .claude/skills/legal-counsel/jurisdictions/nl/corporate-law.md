# Netherlands -- Corporate Law

## Governing Framework

Dutch corporate law is codified in **Boek 2 BW (Rechtspersonen / Legal Entities)**. Key provisions for commercial transactions:

## BV Governance (Art. 2:175--283 BW)

### Bestuurstaak (Duty of Directors, Art. 2:239 BW)

- Directors must act in the interest of the vennootschap en haar onderneming (company and its business)
- Fiduciary duties include: duty of care (zorgplicht), duty of loyalty, duty to act within corporate purpose
- **Bestuurdersaansprakelijkheid (director liability):** personal liability for onbehoorlijke taakvervulling (improper performance) under Art. 2:9 BW; requires ernstig verwijt (serious reproach)

### Tegenstrijdig belang (Conflict of Interest, Art. 2:256 BW)

- Director with direct or indirect personal conflict excluded from decision-making
- Since Flex-BV: no mandatory prohibition on the transaction itself -- governance measure only
- If all directors are conflicted, AVA (general meeting) decides
- **Drafting note:** always include conflict-of-interest procedures in statuten and bestuursinstructie

### Aandeelhoudersvergadering (General Meeting, AVA)

- **Sovereign body:** appoints and dismisses bestuurders (Art. 2:242, 2:244 BW)
- Ontslag bestuurder (dismissal, Art. 2:244 BW): AVA may dismiss at any time; no court intervention needed
- **Besluitvorming buiten vergadering (Art. 2:238 BW):** permitted unless statuten prohibit; requires unanimous written consent
- Key reserved matters (unless varied in statuten): appointment/dismissal directors, amendment of statuten, ontbinding (dissolution), uitgifte van aandelen (share issuance), goedkeuring jaarrekening (approval of annual accounts)

### Aandelenlevering (Share Transfer, Art. 2:196 BW)

- Must be by **notariele akte** + registration in aandeelhoudersregister
- Blokkeringsregeling (Art. 2:195 BW): since Flex-BV, aanbiedingsregeling (right of first offer) is default; freely excludable
- **Leveringsbelemmeringen (transfer restrictions):** lock-up, drag-along, tag-along typically in SHA, not statuten

### Uitkeringen (Distributions, Art. 2:216 BW)

Dual test:
1. **Balanstest:** eigen vermogen (equity) minus distribution exceeds reserves required by statuten
2. **Liquiditeitstest:** bestuurder must approve; personally liable if knew or should have known company could not continue paying debts after distribution

### Verkrijging eigen aandelen (Own Shares, Art. 2:207 BW)

- BV may acquire own shares if eigen vermogen minus verkrijgingsprijs exceeds statutory reserves
- Bestuurder must approve; useful for management buybacks or treasury operations

## NV-Specific Provisions

### Structuurregime (Large Company Regime)

Mandatory for NVs (and large BVs) meeting all three criteria for 3 consecutive years:
- Geplaatst kapitaal + reserves > EUR 16M
- Ondernemingsraad (works council) established
- >100 employees in NL

**Effect:** RvC (supervisory board) mandatory; RvC appoints/dismisses bestuur (not AVA); RvC has veto on certain major decisions (Art. 2:164 BW: major restructuring, significant investment >25% balance sheet, termination of substantial workforce, major management change).

### One-Tier Board (Art. 2:129a / 2:239a BW)

Since 1 January 2013, BVs and NVs may adopt monistisch bestuursstelsel (one-tier board) with executive and non-executive directors. Statuten determine allocation of tasks.

## Herstructurering (Restructuring)

### Juridische fusie (Legal Merger, Art. 2:309 BW)

- Verkrijgende rechtspersoon (acquiring entity) succeeds to all rights and obligations under universele titel
- Requires AVA approval (2/3 majority unless statuten require more)
- Notariele akte + registration at KvK; 1-month verzetsperiode (opposition period) for creditors

### Juridische splitsing (Legal Demerger, Art. 2:334a BW)

- Zuivere splitsing (pure demerger): transferring entity ceases; all assets/liabilities distributed
- Afsplitsing (partial demerger): transferring entity continues; transfers part of assets/liabilities

### Omzetting (Conversion, Art. 2:18 BW)

- Entity may convert to different legal form (e.g., BV to NV) without dissolution
- Requires AVA resolution; notariele akte; certain conversions require unanimous consent

## Jaarrekeningenrecht (Annual Accounts, Art. 2:360 BW)

| Categorie | Criteria | Verplichtingen |
|---|---|---|
| Micro | <EUR 900K balance sheet, <EUR 700K revenue, <10 FTE | Beperkte balans (limited balance sheet) only |
| Klein (small) | <EUR 7.5M balance sheet, <EUR 15M revenue, <50 FTE | Beperkte toelichting; no accountantscontrole required |
| Middelgroot (medium) | <EUR 25M balance sheet, <EUR 50M revenue, <250 FTE | Beperkte toelichting; accountantscontrole required |
| Groot (large) | Exceeds medium thresholds | Volledige jaarrekening; accountantscontrole; bestuursverslag |

- Deponeertermijn (filing deadline): 12 months after boekjaar (financial year end); in practice 8 months (5-month preparation + 2-month adoption + 8-day filing)
- Publicatie (filing) at KvK; failure triggers bestuurdersaansprakelijkheid in faillissement (Art. 2:248 BW)
