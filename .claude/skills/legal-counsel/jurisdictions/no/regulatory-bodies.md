# Norway -- Key Regulatory Bodies and Authorities

## Tax and Revenue

| Myndighet (Authority) | Rolle (Role) |
|---|---|
| Skatteetaten | Norwegian Tax Administration -- primary tax authority for corporate tax, personal tax, MVA, employer obligations. Website: skatteetaten.no |
| Tolletaten | Norwegian Customs Authority -- customs duties, import/export. Website: toll.no |

## Financial Supervision

| Myndighet (Authority) | Rolle (Role) |
|---|---|
| Finanstilsynet | Financial Supervisory Authority -- banks, insurance, securities, fund management, audit, accounting. Website: finanstilsynet.no |
| Norges Bank | Central bank -- monetary policy, financial stability, Government Pension Fund management |

## Business Registration

| Myndighet (Authority) | Rolle (Role) |
|---|---|
| Bronnoysundregistrene | Business registries including Foretaksregisteret (Register of Business Enterprises), Enhetsregisteret (Register of Legal Entities), register over reelle rettighetshavere (UBO register). Website: brreg.no |
| Regnskapsregisteret | Part of Bronnoysundregistrene -- receives annual accounts |

## Digital Government

| Myndighet (Authority) | Rolle (Role) |
|---|---|
| Altinn | Digital portal for all tax filings, business reporting, government correspondence. Website: altinn.no |

## Research and Innovation

| Myndighet (Authority) | Rolle (Role) |
|---|---|
| Norges forskningsrad | Research Council of Norway -- administers SkatteFUNN, research grants. Website: forskningsradet.no |
| Innovasjon Norge | Innovation Norway -- business development, grants, export support. Website: innovasjonnorge.no |

## Labour and Welfare

| Myndighet (Authority) | Rolle (Role) |
|---|---|
| NAV (Arbeids- og velferdsetaten) | Norwegian Labour and Welfare Administration -- unemployment, sickness benefits, pensions. Website: nav.no |
| Arbeidstilsynet | Norwegian Labour Inspection Authority -- workplace safety, working conditions. Website: arbeidstilsynet.no |

## Competition and Energy

| Myndighet (Authority) | Rolle (Role) |
|---|---|
| Konkurransetilsynet | Norwegian Competition Authority. Website: konkurransetilsynet.no |
| NVE (Norges vassdrags- og energidirektorat) | Norwegian Water Resources and Energy Directorate -- energy regulation, grid, concessions. Website: nve.no |
| Statnett | Transmission system operator (TSO) -- national grid. Website: statnett.no |

## Law Enforcement (Financial Crime)

| Myndighet (Authority) | Rolle (Role) |
|---|---|
| Okokrim | National Authority for Investigation and Prosecution of Economic and Environmental Crime -- receives suspicious transaction reports (STR). Website: okokrim.no |
| Datatilsynet | Norwegian Data Protection Authority -- GDPR enforcement. Website: datatilsynet.no |

## Professional Bodies

| Organisasjon (Organization) | Rolle (Role) |
|---|---|
| Norsk RegnskapsStiftelse (NRS) | Standard-setting body for Norwegian GAAP (god regnskapsskikk) |
| Den norske Revisorforening | Norwegian Institute of Public Accountants |
| Regnskap Norge | Association for authorised accounting firms |
| MfN (Mediatorsfederatie) | N/A for Norway; see Advokatforeningen for mediation |
| Den Norske Advokatforening | Norwegian Bar Association |
