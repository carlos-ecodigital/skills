# Norway -- Employment Law

## Primary Sources

- **Arbeidsmiljoloven** (Working Environment Act, aml.) -- Lov 2005-06-17 nr. 62
- **Ferieloven** (Annual Holidays Act) -- Lov 1988-04-29 nr. 21
- **Folketrygdloven** (National Insurance Act, ftrl.) -- Lov 1997-02-28 nr. 19
- **Likestillings- og diskrimineringsloven** (Equality and Anti-Discrimination Act) -- Lov 2017-06-16 nr. 51
- **Allmenngjoringsvedtak** (extension of collective agreements to non-unionised workers in certain sectors)

## Employment Relationship

### Ansettelse (Employment)
- **Arbeidsavtale** (employment contract): must be in writing -- aml. § 14-5
- Default: fast ansettelse (permanent employment) -- aml. § 14-9
- **Midlertidig ansettelse** (temporary employment): only permitted in specific circumstances -- aml. § 14-9(2):
  - Work of a temporary nature
  - Vikariat (substitution for absent employee)
  - Praksisarbeid (traineeship)
  - Up to 12 months if agreed (general basis) -- max 15% of workforce
- **Provetid** (probation period): max 6 months -- aml. § 15-6

### Arbeidstid (Working Hours)
- Normal: 40 hours/week (37.5 hours under most tariffavtaler / collective agreements)
- Overtid (overtime): max 10 hours/week, 25 hours/4 weeks, 200 hours/year -- aml. § 10-6
- Overtime compensation: minimum 40% premium -- aml. § 10-6(11)

## Oppsigelse (Termination)

### Oppsigelsesfrister (Notice Periods)
- During provetid: 14 days
- Standard minimum: 1 month (if no tariffavtale / collective agreement)
- Statutory minimums based on tenure and age:

| Ansiennitet (Tenure) | Frist (Notice) | Ekstra for alder 50+ |
|---|---|---|
| <5 years | 1 month | -- |
| 5-10 years | 2 months | 50+: 4 months |
| 10+ years | 3 months | 55+: 5 months; 60+: 6 months |

### Saklig grunn (Just Cause) -- aml. § 15-7
- Termination must be **saklig begrunnet** (objectively justified) based on:
  - **Virksomhetens forhold** (business reasons): downsizing, restructuring, relocation
  - **Arbeidstakers forhold** (employee reasons): poor performance, breach of duties, absence
- Droftelsesplikt (duty to consult): employer must consult with employee before giving notice -- aml. § 15-1
- Formkrav (formal requirements): written notice with specified content -- aml. § 15-4

### Avskjed (Summary Dismissal) -- aml. § 15-14
- Immediate termination for grovt pliktbrudd (gross breach of duty) or annet vesentlig mislighold (other material default)
- Very high threshold; rarely upheld by courts

### Nedbemanning (Redundancy)
- Social criteria for selecting employees: ansiennitet (seniority), kompetanse (skills), sosiale forhold (social circumstances)
- Tariffavtaler may impose additional requirements (e.g., LO/NHO Hovedavtalen: seniority as primary criterion)
- **Masseoppsigelse** (collective redundancy): if 10+ employees within 30 days -- aml. § 15-2:
  - Droftinger med tillitsvalgte (consultation with employee representatives)
  - Notification to NAV (Norwegian Labour and Welfare Administration)
  - 30-day cooling-off period before first dismissal

### Fortrinnsrett (Preferential Right to Re-Employment) -- aml. § 14-2
- Employees dismissed for business reasons have preferential right to new positions for 12 months
- Applies if employee is qualified for the position

## Sluttvederlag / Sluttavtale (Severance / Settlement Agreement)

- No statutory severance requirement (unlike some jurisdictions)
- Sluttavtale (settlement agreement) commonly negotiated: typically 3-12 months salary + benefits
- Settlement must be voluntary; employee should have time to consider and seek advice
- Tax treatment: sluttvederlag taxable as employment income; fratreden (gardening leave) taxable as salary

## Konkurranseklausul (Non-Compete) -- aml. kap. 14A

- **Duration:** max 12 months after employment ends -- aml. § 14A-1
- **Compensation:** employer must pay min. 100% of salary up to 8G (G = grunnbelopet, ca. NOK 124,028 in 2025), then 70% of salary above 8G, for the restriction period -- aml. § 14A-3
- **Formkrav:** must be in writing and employer must confirm enforcement and duration before or at termination
- **Kundeklausul** (non-solicitation of clients): max 12 months; must specify which clients -- aml. § 14A-4
- **Rekrutteringsklausul** (non-solicitation of employees): may be agreed without compensation -- aml. § 14A-6

## Ferie (Annual Leave) -- Ferieloven

- Standard entitlement: 25 virkedager (working days) = 4 weeks + 1 day
- Tariffavtale: many collective agreements provide 5 weeks (30 virkedager)
- Over 60: additional 6 virkedager (1 extra week)
- Feriepenger (holiday pay): 10.2% of previous year's gross salary (12.0% under 5-week schemes; 14.3% for over 60)

## Arbeidsgiveravgift (Employer NIC) -- See `tax-framework.md`

Zone-based employer NIC from 0% to 14.1% + 5% surcharge on salaries >NOK 850,000 (2025) / >NOK 950,000 (2026).
