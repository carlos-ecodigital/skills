# Regnskapsstandarder (Accounting Standards) -- Norwegian Accounting Standards Guide

## Hjemmel (Legal Basis)

- Regnskapsloven (Accounting Act) -- Lov 1998-07-17 nr. 56
- Norsk RegnskapsStiftelse (NRS) -- standard-setting body for Norwegian GAAP

---

## Rammeverk (Framework Overview)

Three accounting frameworks apply in Norway. The applicable framework depends on the entity type and size:

| Rammeverk (Framework) | Gjelder for (Applies to) | Hjemmel (Basis) |
|---|---|---|
| IFRS (full) | Bornoterte selskaper (listed companies) -- mandatory | EU-forordning (regulation) via EOS-avtalen |
| Forenklet IFRS (simplified IFRS) | Non-listed entities -- optional election | Regnskapsloven § 3-9, forskrift 2014 |
| God regnskapsskikk / GRS (Norwegian GAAP) | All other regnskapspliktige entities -- default | Regnskapsloven, NRS-standarder |

### Valg av rammeverk (Choice of Framework)
- Bornoterte selskaper (listed companies): IFRS is obligatorisk (mandatory) for konsernregnskapet (consolidated accounts); may use GRS or forenklet IFRS for selskapsregnskapet (individual accounts)
- Non-listed entities: choose between GRS and forenklet IFRS; election must be consistent across all standards (cannot cherry-pick)
- Sma foretak (small entities): may use simplified GRS rules under NRS 8

---

## Norsk RegnskapsStiftelse (NRS)

### Organisasjon (Organization)
- Established in 1989 as an independent standard-setting body
- Issues norske regnskapsstandarder (Norwegian accounting standards) -- authoritative guidance on god regnskapsskikk (GRS)
- Members include Den norske Revisorforening (Norwegian Institute of Public Accountants), Regnskap Norge, NHO, Finans Norge, Oslo Bors

### Standardtyper (Standard Types)
- **NRS (endelig standard):** Final standards -- authoritative and binding for GRS reporting
- **NRS (forelopig standard):** Preliminary standards -- carry significant weight but may be revised
- **NRS (veiledning):** Guidance -- supplementary, not mandatory

## NRS-standarder (Norwegian Accounting Standards) -- Oversikt / Overview

| Standard | Tittel (Title) | Gjelder (Scope) |
|---|---|---|
| NRS 1 | Varer (Inventories) | Verdsettelse (valuation) and presentation of varebeholdning (inventory) |
| NRS 2 | Anleggskontrakter (Construction Contracts) | Inntektsforting (revenue recognition) for long-term contracts using lopendeberegningsmetoden (percentage of completion) or fullfort kontraktsmetode (completed contract) |
| NRS 4 | Betingede utfall og hendelser etter balansedagen (Contingencies and Events After the Balance Sheet Date) | Recognition and disclosure of betingede forpliktelser (contingent liabilities) and betingede eiendeler (contingent assets) |
| NRS 5 | Ekstraordinaere inntekter og kostnader (Extraordinary Items) | Classification of unusual or infrequent items -- note: largely aligned with IFRS elimination of this category |
| NRS 6 | Pensjonskostnader (Pension Costs) | Regnskapsforing (accounting) for ytelsesbaserte (defined benefit) and innskuddsbaserte (defined contribution) pensjonsordninger (pension plans) |
| NRS 8 | God regnskapsskikk for sma foretak (Good Accounting Practice for Small Entities) | Comprehensive simplified standard for sma foretak meeting regnskapsloven § 1-6 criteria; covers all topics in one standard |
| NRS 9 | Fusjon (Mergers) | Accounting for legal mergers (fusjoner) including valuation and classification |
| NRS 13 | Usikre forpliktelser og betingede eiendeler (Provisions, Contingent Liabilities and Contingent Assets) | Similar to IAS 37; recognition criteria, measurement, and disclosure |
| NRS 14 | Leieavtaler (Leases) | Classification as finansiell leie (finance lease) or operasjonell leie (operating lease); note: GRS has not adopted IFRS 16 single-model approach |
| NRS 15 | Implementering av IAS 19 Ytelser til ansatte (Implementation of IAS 19 Employee Benefits) | Guidance on applying IAS 19 principles within GRS framework |
| NRS 16 | Arsberetning (Directors' Report) | Content requirements for arsberetningen including going concern, risk factors, forward-looking statements |
| NRS 17 | Virksomhetssammenslutninger og konsernregnskap (Business Combinations and Consolidated Financial Statements) | Oppkjopsmetoden (acquisition method), goodwill, minoritetsinteresser (minority interests), konsolideringsprinsipper |
| NRS 18 | Endringer i regnskapsprinsipper, estimater, og korrigering av feil (Changes in Accounting Policies, Estimates, and Correction of Errors) | Retrospective vs prospective application, error correction |
| NRS 19 | Immaterielle eiendeler (Intangible Assets) | Recognition, measurement, and amortization of patent, lisenser (licenses), varemerker (trademarks), utviklingskostnader (development costs) |
| NRS 20 | Transaksjoner og regnskap i utenlandsk valuta (Foreign Currency Transactions and Translation) | Transaction date recognition, balansedagskurs (closing rate) revaluation, omregningsdifferanser (translation differences) |

### Viktige forskjeller mellom GRS og IFRS (Key Differences Between GRS and IFRS)

| Omrade (Area) | GRS | IFRS |
|---|---|---|
| Leieavtaler (Leases) | Two-model: finance vs operating (NRS 14) | Single model: all leases on balance sheet (IFRS 16) |
| Inntektsforting (Revenue recognition) | Transaction-based (rskl. § 4-1) | IFRS 15 five-step model |
| Goodwill | Amortized over useful life (max 20 years) | Annual impairment test, no amortization (IAS 36/IFRS 3) |
| Utviklingskostnader (Development costs) | May be expensed or capitalized | Must capitalize if criteria met (IAS 38) |
| Finansielle instrumenter (Financial instruments) | Historic cost with some exceptions | Fair value through profit/loss or OCI (IFRS 9) |
| Pensjon (Pensions) | Options for actuarial gains/losses | Immediate recognition in OCI (IAS 19R) |

### NS 4102 og SAF-T (Relationship to SAF-T)
- SAF-T Financial requires mapping of the company's kontoplan (chart of accounts) to standard NS 4102 account ranges
- The mapping ensures Skatteetaten can analyze financial data consistently across different accounting systems
- Companies using customized kontoplaner must maintain a mapping table (kontokart) between their internal accounts and NS 4102
- SAF-T export includes generell hovedbok (general ledger), kundereskontro (accounts receivable), leverandorreskontro (accounts payable), and MVA-detaljer (VAT details)

## CSRD / Barekraftsrapportering (Sustainability Reporting)

### Bakgrunn (Background)
The EU Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD) is implemented in Norway through regnskapsloven amendments.

### Implementeringsplan (Implementation Timeline)

| Faseinn (Phase-in) | Gjelder for (Applies to) | Forste rapporteriingsar (First reporting year) |
|---|---|---|
| Fase 1 | Store foretak av allmenn interesse (large PIEs) with > 500 ansatte | Regnskapsaret (fiscal year) 2024 |
| Fase 2 | Andre store foretak (other large entities) meeting 2 of 3 criteria | Regnskapsaret 2025 |
| Fase 3 | Bornoterte SMB, sma kreditinstitusjoner, captive forsikringsforetak (listed SMEs, small credit institutions, captive insurance) | Regnskapsaret 2026 (opt-out until 2028) |

### Krav (Requirements)
- Report in accordance with European Sustainability Reporting Standards (ESRS)
- Dobbel vesentlighetsanalyse (double materiality assessment): both impact materiality and financial materiality
- Integration in arsberetningen (directors' report) or separate barekraftsrapport (sustainability report)
- Tredjepartsattestasjon (third-party assurance): limited assurance initially, with transition to reasonable assurance
- Maskinlesbart format (machine-readable format): XBRL tagging required
