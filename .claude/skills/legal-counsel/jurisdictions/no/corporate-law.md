# Norway -- Corporate Law

## Primary Sources

- **Aksjeloven** (Private Limited Companies Act, asl.) -- Lov 1997-06-13 nr. 44
- **Allmennaksjeloven** (Public Limited Companies Act, asal.) -- Lov 1997-06-13 nr. 45
- **Selskapsloven** (Partnership Act, sel.) -- Lov 1985-06-21 nr. 83

## Governance -- AS (Aksjeselskap)

### Styre (Board of Directors)
- **Composition:** minimum 1 member (capital <NOK 3M) or 3 members (capital >=NOK 3M) -- asl. § 6-1
- **Employee representation:** mandatory if >30 employees (1/3 of board); if >50 employees, may also establish bedriftsforsamling (corporate assembly) -- asl. § 6-4, § 6-35
- **Styreleder (chair):** elected by generalforsamling or by the board -- asl. § 6-1
- **Board duties:** forvaltning (management) and tilsyn (supervision) of the company -- asl. § 6-12
- **Inhabilitetsregler (conflict of interest):** board member may not participate in decisions involving own personal interest -- asl. § 6-27

### Daglig leder (Managing Director / CEO)
- Not mandatory for AS with capital <NOK 3M (mandatory for ASA)
- Manages day-to-day operations within the board's instructions -- asl. § 6-14
- Reports to the board; board may instruct or overrule daglig leder

### Generalforsamling (General Meeting)
- **Ordinaer generalforsamling (AGM):** within 6 months after year-end -- asl. § 5-5
- **Mandatory agenda items:** godkjenne arsregnskapet (approve annual accounts), vedta utbytte (resolve dividends)
- **Vedtektsendring (articles amendment):** 2/3 majority of both votes and represented capital -- asl. § 5-18
- **Beslutning om fusjon/fisjon (merger/demerger):** 2/3 majority -- asl. §§ 13-3, 14-6
- **Opplosning (dissolution):** 2/3 majority -- asl. § 16-1

### Aksjonaerrettigheter (Shareholder Rights)
- **Utbyttekrav** (dividend claims): shareholder cannot demand dividend; board proposes, GM decides -- asl. § 8-2
- **Minoritetsvern** (minority protection):
  - 10%+ can demand ekstraordinaer generalforsamling (extraordinary GM) -- asl. § 5-7
  - 10%+ can demand granskning (investigation) via court -- asl. § 5-25
  - Any shareholder can challenge GM resolutions that violate law or vedtekter -- asl. § 5-22
- **Innlosning** (redemption): shareholder may claim innlosning if oppressed -- asl. § 4-24
- **Utlosning** (compulsory acquisition by majority): 90%+ shareholder may squeeze out minority -- asl. § 4-26

## Share Transfers

- **Default rule:** board samtykke (consent) required for share transfers -- asl. § 4-15(2)
- Board can only refuse on saklig grunnlag (reasonable grounds)
- **Vedtektsfestet forkjopsrett** (statutory/articles pre-emption): may be included in vedtektene -- asl. § 4-19
- **Aksjonaerbok** (shareholders' register): maintained by the company; transfers recorded upon notification -- asl. § 4-7
- No notarial requirement for share transfers (unlike Dutch BV)

## Distributions

### Utbytte (Dividends) -- asl. § 8-1
- May only be distributed from fri egenkapital (distributable reserves)
- Distributable reserves = net profit + retained earnings - losses - bound reserves
- **Forsvarlighetsvurdering** (prudence assessment): board must ensure company retains adequate egenkapital (equity) and likviditet (liquidity) -- asl. § 3-4
- Tilleggsutbytte (interim/supplementary dividend): may be declared after AGM based on interim accounts -- asl. § 8-2a

### Konsernbidrag (Group Contributions) -- sktl. §§ 10-2 to 10-4
- Tax mechanism for transferring income between 90%+ owned group companies
- Fradragsberettiget (deductible) for giver, skattepliktig (taxable) for receiver
- See `tax-framework.md` for details

## Restructuring

### Fusjon (Merger) -- asl. kap. 13
- Types: absorpsjonsfusjon (merger by absorption), nystiftelsesfusjon (merger by new formation)
- Kreditorfrist (creditor protection period): 6 weeks from announcement
- Skattefri (tax-free) if conditions in sktl. §§ 11-1 ff. met (skattemessig kontinuitet / tax continuity)

### Fisjon (Demerger) -- asl. kap. 14
- Types: fullstendig fisjon (full demerger), partiell fisjon (partial demerger)
- Same creditor protection and tax continuity rules as merger

### Aksjebytte (Share Exchange) -- sktl. § 11-11(4)
- Tax-free exchange of shares if requirements met
- Must result in 100% ownership post-exchange (for Norwegian targets)

### Omdanning (Conversion)
- AS may convert to ASA and vice versa -- asl. § 15-1
- ENK may convert to AS (tax-free if conditions met)
