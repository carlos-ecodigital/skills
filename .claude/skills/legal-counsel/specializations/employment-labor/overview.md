# Employment & Labor -- Specialization Overview

> **Status:** Phase 4f stub. To be expanded with full content covering employment relationships, executive compensation, and workforce restructuring.

## Scope

This specialization covers **universal employment law concepts**. Jurisdiction-specific employment rules live in jurisdiction files (e.g., `nl/employment-law.md`, `no/employment-law.md`).

- **Employment relationships** (employee vs contractor, fixed-term vs permanent, secondment)
- **Executive compensation** (ESOP, VSOP, phantom equity, SAR, carried interest)
- **Termination** (grounds, notice periods, severance, settlement agreements)
- **Workforce restructuring** (collective redundancy, TUPE/business transfers, works councils)
- **Non-compete / non-solicit** (enforceability, blue-pencil, garden leave)
- **International employment** (posted workers, A1 certificates, social security coordination)

## Key Concepts

### Employment vs Independent Contractor
| Factor | Employee | Contractor |
|---|---|---|
| Control | Employer directs work | Self-directed |
| Integration | Part of organisation | Own business |
| Economic dependence | Single employer | Multiple clients |
| Tools/equipment | Employer-provided | Own |
| Risk | Employer bears | Contractor bears |
| Tax/social security | Employer withholds | Self-assessed |

### Equity Compensation Structures
| Type | Ownership? | Tax Event | Typical Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Stock options (ESOP) | Yes (on exercise) | Exercise | Startups, listed companies |
| Restricted stock | Yes (subject to vesting) | Grant/vesting | Executive incentive |
| Phantom equity (VSOP) | No | Payment | Private companies, tax efficiency |
| SAR | No | Payment | Cash-settled incentive |
| Carried interest | Varies | Realisation | Fund/PE structures |
