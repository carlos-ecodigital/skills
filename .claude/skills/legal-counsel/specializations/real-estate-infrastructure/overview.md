## Scope

This specialization covers:

## Key Concepts

### Construction Contract Comparison
| Standard | Origin | Risk Allocation | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| UAV 2012 | NL | Traditional (design by employer) | Standard NL construction |
| UAV-GC 2005 | NL | Design & build (integrated) | NL integrated contracts |
| FIDIC Red | International | Traditional | International, employer-designed |
| FIDIC Yellow | International | Design & build | International D&B |
| FIDIC Silver | International | EPC/turnkey (most risk to contractor) | Lump-sum, project finance |
| NEC4 | UK | Collaborative, pain/gain share | UK infrastructure |
| JCT | UK | Traditional | UK building works |

### Property Transaction Lifecycle
1. **Heads of terms** -- key commercial terms, exclusivity
2. **Due diligence** -- title, planning, environmental, tenant review
3. **Contract** -- conditional/unconditional, deposit, long-stop date
4. **Completion** -- notarial deed (NL), registration, funds transfer
5. **Post-completion** -- stamp duty/transfer tax, tenant notification, insurance
