# Dispute Resolution -- Specialization Overview

> **Status:** Phase 4b stub. To be expanded with full content covering litigation, arbitration, mediation, and enforcement.

## Scope

This specialization covers:

- **Litigation** (court proceedings, provisional measures, summary proceedings)
- **Arbitration** (institutional rules: ICC, LCIA, AAA, NAI, NCC/OCC, SIAC)
- **Mediation** (voluntary/mandatory, MII/IMI accreditation, settlement facilitation)
- **Expert determination** (technical disputes, valuation disputes, binding opinions)
- **Settlement agreements** (structuring, confidentiality, tax treatment, enforcement)
- **Enforcement** (domestic judgments, foreign judgments, arbitral awards, NY Convention)

## Key Concepts

### Dispute Resolution Spectrum
| Method | Binding? | Confidential? | Typical Duration | Cost Level |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Negotiation | No | Yes | Weeks | Low |
| Mediation | No (unless settled) | Yes | 1-3 months | Low-Medium |
| Expert determination | Yes (usually) | Yes | 2-4 months | Medium |
| Arbitration | Yes | Yes | 6-18 months | High |
| Litigation | Yes | No (public) | 12-36 months | High |

### Clause Drafting Considerations
- Multi-tier clauses (negotiate > mediate > arbitrate/litigate)
- Seat vs venue vs governing law distinction
- Emergency arbitrator provisions
- Joinder and consolidation in multi-party disputes
- Carve-outs for IP, injunctive relief, summary proceedings
