# Tax & VAT -- Specialization Overview

> **Status:** Phase 4c stub. To be expanded with full content covering international tax planning, BEPS, VAT, and treaty utilization.

## Scope

This specialization covers **universal tax concepts and cross-border planning**. Jurisdiction-specific rates, rules, and compliance live in the jurisdiction files (e.g., `nl/tax-framework.md`, `no/tax-framework.md`).

- **Corporate income tax** (international structuring, BEPS, Pillar Two, anti-abuse rules)
- **VAT / indirect tax** (cross-border supply, reverse charge, place of supply, exemptions)
- **Withholding tax** (dividends, interest, royalties, treaty relief, beneficial ownership)
- **Transfer pricing** (arm's length, OECD guidelines, documentation, APAs)
- **Tax treaty planning** (LOB, PPT, treaty shopping, beneficial ownership, PE risk)
- **Transaction tax** (real estate transfer tax, stamp duty, securities transaction tax)

## Key Concepts

### International Tax Framework
| Concept | Description | Key Source |
|---|---|---|
| Pillar One | Reallocation of taxing rights to market jurisdictions | OECD/IF |
| Pillar Two | Global minimum tax 15% (GloBE rules, QDMTT, IIR, UTPR) | EU Directive 2022/2523 |
| ATAD I/II | Anti-avoidance: interest limitation, CFC, exit tax, GAAR, hybrid mismatches | EU Directives |
| MLI | Multilateral instrument modifying bilateral treaties | OECD MLI |
| DAC6/DAC7 | Mandatory disclosure rules for cross-border arrangements | EU Directives |

### Cross-Border VAT Principles
- Place of supply rules (B2B reverse charge vs B2C destination)
- Chain transactions and triangulation
- Fixed establishment concept
- Import VAT deferral schemes
- Digital services (OSS/IOSS)
