# Regulatory & Compliance -- Specialization Overview

> **Status:** Phase 4g stub. To be expanded with full content covering compliance programs, AML/KYC, and ESG/sustainability reporting.

## Scope

This specialization covers:

- **Compliance program design** (risk assessment, policies, training, monitoring, reporting)
- **AML/KYC** (customer due diligence, UBO identification, sanctions screening, SAR)
- **ESG & sustainability** (CSRD, EU Taxonomy, SFDR, CSDDD, double materiality)
- **Sanctions** (EU, US OFAC, UK, UN, screening obligations, licensing)
- **Anti-bribery / anti-corruption** (FCPA, UK Bribery Act, Dutch law, facilitation payments)
- **Whistleblowing** (EU Whistleblower Directive, Huis voor Klokkenluiders, internal procedures)

## Key Concepts

### Compliance Program Elements
1. **Risk assessment** -- identify, assess, and prioritize compliance risks
2. **Policies & procedures** -- code of conduct, AML policy, sanctions policy, gift policy
3. **Training & awareness** -- role-based, annual refresher, scenario exercises
4. **Monitoring & testing** -- transaction monitoring, periodic reviews, audits
5. **Reporting** -- internal escalation, SAR/UTR, regulatory reporting
6. **Remediation** -- root cause analysis, disciplinary action, process improvement

### ESG Reporting Landscape
| Framework | Scope | Mandatory? | Key Feature |
|---|---|---|---|
| CSRD | EU large + listed companies | Yes (phased 2024-2028) | Double materiality, ESRS standards |
| EU Taxonomy | Taxonomy-eligible activities | Yes (for CSRD reporters) | 6 environmental objectives, DNSH |
| SFDR | Financial market participants | Yes | Fund-level sustainability disclosure |
| CSDDD | Large EU companies | Yes (phased 2027-2029) | Supply chain human rights/environmental DD |
| GRI | Any organisation | Voluntary | Impact materiality |
| ISSB (IFRS S1/S2) | Any organisation | Varies by jurisdiction | Financial materiality |
