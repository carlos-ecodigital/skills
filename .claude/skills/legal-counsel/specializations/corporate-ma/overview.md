# Corporate & M&A -- Specialization Overview

> **Status:** Phase 4a stub. To be expanded with full content covering deal lifecycle, structures, due diligence, and corporate restructuring.

## Scope

This specialization covers:

- **Share acquisitions** (SPA structuring, W&I, completion mechanics, locked-box vs completion accounts)
- **Asset acquisitions** (business transfer, successor liability, contract novation)
- **Joint ventures** (see `jv-structuring.md` for detailed JV governance and structuring)
- **Mergers and demergers** (statutory merger, juridische fusie/splitsing, cross-border)
- **Corporate restructuring** (squeeze-out, share buyback, capital reduction, conversions)
- **Due diligence** (legal, financial, tax, technical, environmental, commercial)
- **Post-completion** (integration, earn-out disputes, warranty claims, escrow releases)

## Key Concepts

### Deal Lifecycle
1. **Origination** -- target identification, indicative offer, exclusivity
2. **Due diligence** -- vendor/buyer DD, red flag vs full scope, virtual data room
3. **Negotiation** -- SPA/SHA markup, W&I insurance, disclosure process
4. **Signing** -- conditions precedent, regulatory approvals, antitrust filing
5. **Completion** -- closing deliverables, funds flow, post-completion adjustments
6. **Post-completion** -- earn-out, warranty window, integration

### Common Structures
| Structure | Use Case | Key Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Share deal | Acquire entire entity | Successor to all liabilities |
| Asset deal | Cherry-pick assets | Transfer tax, contract consent |
| Merger | Combine two entities | Minority squeeze-out threshold |
| Demerger | Separate business lines | Creditor protection period |
| JV | Shared ownership/operation | Deadlock, exit triggers |
