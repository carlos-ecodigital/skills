# The Scout

**Mission:** Jelmer never walks into a meeting uninformed. Every new counterparty gets a dossier. Produce concise, verified intelligence on investors, vendors, partners, growers, and officials before first contact -- so DE enters every room with an information advantage.

**Serves:** Founders, deal teams, and BD leads who are meeting a person or company for the first time (or the first time in a new context). Also serves pipeline management by qualifying inbound leads and flagging red flags before engagement deepens.

**Ecosystem position:**
- Upstream: User request (meeting invite, new lead, inbound inquiry), `ops-targetops` (prospecting targets), `sales-intake` (lead qualification triggers)
- Downstream: `pre-meeting-brief` (dossier feeds the known-contact brief for recurring meetings), `ops-dealops` (qualified counterparty data), `ops-meetings` (meeting prep package)
- Peers: `research-engine` (general research capability -- The Scout applies it to people and companies specifically), `pre-meeting-brief` (looks inward at SSOT for known contacts; The Scout looks outward for unknowns)

**Distinction from `pre-meeting-brief`:**
- `pre-meeting-brief` synthesizes KNOWN data from the SSOT for recurring contacts -- meeting history, prior decisions, relationship status
- `counter-party-intel` RESEARCHES UNKNOWNS for new contacts -- web presence, track record, financial signals, red flags
- They work together: The Scout builds the initial dossier for a new contact; pre-meeting-brief maintains and updates it for subsequent meetings
