# Grid Connection and Energy Permits in the Netherlands

## 1. Legislative Framework

### Energiewet (New Energy Act)
The Energiewet replaces the Elektriciteitswet 1998 (E-wet / Electricity Act) and the Gaswet (Gas Act). It provides the legal framework for the energietransitie (energy system transition).

**Timeline:**
- 4 June 2024: Adopted by Tweede Kamer (Lower House)
- 22 February 2025: Partial early implementation (amended E-wet 1998: aansluitplicht / connection obligation changed to "redelijke termijn" / reasonable term)
- 1 July 2025: Gegevensuitwisseling (data sharing) provisions active
- 1 January 2026: Primary entry into force (via uitvoeringsbesluiten / implementing decrees)

**Core Objectives:**
- Solid foundation for energietransitie and safe, reliable, affordable energievoorziening (energy supply)
- Unified framework replacing two separate energy laws
- Enhanced consumentenbescherming (consumer protection) and data rights
- Expanded role for ACM (Autoriteit Consument & Markt / Authority for Consumers and Markets) regulation
- Legal basis for cable pooling, energiegemeenschappen (energy communities), flexibele contracten (flexible contracts)

### Key Terminology Changes
- "Netbeheer" (grid management) becomes "systeembeheer" (system management)
- Landelijk net / national grid (> 110 kV) = transmissiesysteem (transmission system)
- Regionaal net / regional grid (< 110 kV) = distributiesysteem (distribution system)
- TenneT = landelijk systeembeheerder (national system manager / TSO)
- Liander/Stedin/Enexis = regionaal systeembeheerder (regional system manager / DSO)

## 2. Grid Congestion Crisis (Transportschaarste / Transport Scarcity)

### Current Status (2025-2026)
The Netherlands faces one of Europe's most severe netcongestie (grid congestion) crises:

- **10,000+ grote verbruikers (large consumers)** (consumers or batteries) on wachtlijsten (waiting lists) for new/heavier aansluitingen (connections)
- **7,500 opwekprojecten (generation projects)** (> household-scale PV) waiting for aansluiting (connection)
- **Wachttijden (wait times): up to 10 years** in worst-affected areas
- Elektriciteitsnet (electricity grid) at maximum capacity during piekuren (peak hours) **almost everywhere** in the Netherlands

### Regional Congestion Hotspots

| Regio (Region) | Status | Netbeheerder (Grid Operator) |
|---|---|---|
| Zuid-Holland | Vol capaciteit (full capacity) announced Dec 2024 (HS / HV) | TenneT + Stedin |
| Utrecht | Vol capaciteit announced Feb 2025 (HS) | TenneT + Stedin |
| Den Haag region | Regionale netcongestie (regional grid congestion) Mar 2025 | Stedin |
| Noord-Holland (Amsterdam) | Ongoing capaciteitsbeperkingen (capacity constraints) | TenneT + Liander |
| Brabant | Severe congestion, especially invoeding (feed-in) | TenneT + Enexis |
| Overijssel/Gelderland | Growing congestion | TenneT + Liander/Enexis |
| Friesland/Groningen | Invoedingscongestie (feed-in congestion) from wind/solar | TenneT + Enexis/Liander |

### Check Current Status
- Capaciteitskaart (capacity map): capaciteitskaart.netbeheernederland.nl
- Shows real-time beschikbare capaciteit (available capacity) per regio and per station
- Separate maps for afname (consumption/offtake) and invoeding (feed-in/injection)
- Updated regularly by netbeheerders (grid operators)

## 3. Grid Connection Process

### 3.1 TenneT (Transmissie / Transmission: HS/EHS > 110 kV)

**Typical Applicants:** Large data centers, utility-scale BESS, large industrial facilities, offshore windaansluiting (wind connection)

**Process:**
1. **Oriënterend gesprek (orientation meeting):** discuss project, capaciteitsbehoefte (capacity needs), timing
2. **Aanvraag aansluiting (connection application):** formal application with technische specificaties (technical specifications)
3. **Haalbaarheidsstudie TenneT (TenneT feasibility study):** TenneT assesses netcapaciteit (grid capacity)
4. **Offerte/aanbod (offer):** TenneT provides connection offer with kostenraming (cost estimate) and planning (timeline)
5. **Aansluit- en transportovereenkomst (ATO / connection and transport agreement):** binding agreement for aansluiting and transport
6. **Realisatie (realization):** ontwerp (design), inkoop (procurement), bouw (construction) of connection infrastructure
7. **Inbedrijfstelling (commissioning):** testing and inschakelmoment (energization)

**Timeline:** 2-10+ years depending on location, capacity, and required netverzwaring (grid reinforcement)

**Cost:** Customer pays aansluitkosten (connection costs) per TenneT's tariefstructuur (tariff structure). Transportkosten (transport costs) are gesocialiseerd (socialized) via transporttarieven (transport tariffs) set by ACM.

### 3.2 DSO (Distributie / Distribution: < 110 kV)

**Operators:** Liander, Stedin, Enexis (regionale monopolisten / regional monopolies)

**Process:**
1. **Aanvraag (application)** via netbeheerder's online portaal (portal)
2. **Beoordeling (assessment):** capaciteitstoets (capacity check), technische haalbaarheid (technical feasibility)
3. **Offerte (offer)** with kostenraming and verwachte leverdatum (expected delivery date)
4. **Overeenkomst (agreement):** aansluitovereenkomst (connection agreement) + transportovereenkomst (transport agreement)
5. **Realisatie (realization):** connection construction
6. **Aansluiting (connection):** meterinstallatie (meter installation), inschakeling (energization)

**Typical Timeline:**
- Kleine aansluitingen (small connections, 3x25A - 3x80A): 18 weken (weeks, wettelijke termijn / statutory deadline)
- Grote aansluitingen (large connections, > 3x80A): individually assessed, often 1-5+ years
- In congestiegebieden (congested areas): placed on wachtlijst (waiting list)

### 3.3 Aansluitplicht (Connection Obligation) Changes

**Old Rule (E-wet 1998, pre-Feb 2025):**
- Netbeheerder had unconditional aansluitplicht (connection obligation)
- Must connect within "redelijke termijn" (reasonable term)
- Redelijke termijn not clearly defined; led to geschillen (disputes)

**New Rule (E-wet 1998 amendment, 22 Feb 2025; continued in Energiewet):**
- Netbeheerder is "niet langer verplicht om aansluitingen onmiddellijk te realiseren als er geen transportcapaciteit beschikbaar is" (no longer obliged to realise connections immediately if no transport capacity is available)
- Aansluitplicht remains but is conditioned on capaciteitsbeschikbaarheid (capacity availability)
- Wachtlijstbeheer (waiting list management) formalized
- Recht op vaste aansluiting (right to firm connection / vast transportrecht) becomes a premiumproduct (premium product)

## 4. Congestion Management Solutions

### 4.1 TenneT Flexibele Contracten (Flexible Contracts)

**Tijdgebonden Transportrechten (TBTR / Time-Bound Transmission Rights) — April 2025:**
- TenneT identified 9.1 GW available outside piekuren (peak hours) on HS-net (HV grid)
- Offered via allocatieprocedure (allocation procedure) to interested parties
- Applications exceeded 70 GW (mainly from batterijparken / battery parks)
- Enables BESS and flexibele verbruikers (flexible consumers) to connect faster
- Capacity only usable during daluren (off-peak periods)

**Niet-vast Transportrecht (Non-Firm Transport Rights):**
- Connection with afschakelrisico (curtailment risk) during peak congestion
- Lower cost than vast transportrecht (firm transport rights)
- Suitable for flexibele belastingen (flexible loads): BESS, elektrolysers (electrolyzers), data centers with vraagsturing (load management)

### 4.2 Congestiemanagement (Congestion Management)

**Spitstroken / Flex-regeling (Flex Arrangement):**
- Netbeheerder contracts with aangeslotenen (connected parties) to reduce belasting (load) during piek (peak)
- Vergoeding (compensation) for afgeschakelde productie of verbruik (curtailed production or consumption)
- Verplichte deelname (mandatory participation) for grote aansluitingen in congestiegebieden
- Based on merit order (goedkoopste flexibiliteit eerst / cheapest flexibility first)

### 4.3 Prioritering (Prioritization)

**LAN (Landelijk Actieprogramma Netcongestie / National Action Program Grid Congestion):**
- National action program with 100+ gerichte maatregelen (targeted measures)
- Published March 2025 (second voortgangsrapportage / progress report)
- Key measure: replacing "recht op aansluiting" (right to connection) with "recht op vaste aansluiting" (right to firm connection) as premiumproduct
- Treating 24/7 gegarandeerde capaciteit (guaranteed capacity) at transmission level as premium product
- Verplichte flexibiliteit (mandatory flexibility) for new grote aansluitingen

### 4.4 Netverzwaring (Grid Reinforcement)

**TenneT Investeringen (Investments):**
- EUR 235 billion planned investment through 2050
- 100,000 km new kabels (cables) to be laid
- Timeline reality: ~8 years wetgeving/vergunningen (legislation/permitting) + ~2 years bouw (construction) per project
- Priorities: Delta Rijn Corridor (Delta Rhine Corridor), offshore windaansluitingen (wind connections), industriële clusters (industrial cluster supply)

**DSO Investeringen:**
- Major investeringsprogramma's (capital programs) by Liander, Stedin, Enexis
- Onderstationuitbreiding (transformer station expansion)
- Kabelvervanging (cable replacements) and capaciteitsverhoging (capacity upgrades)
- Arbeids- en materiaaltekorten (labor and material shortages) limiting pace

## 5. Cable Pooling

### Definition
Cable pooling allows different installaties (installations) to share a single netaansluiting (grid connection), reducing the need for separate connections and making more efficient use of existing netcapaciteit (grid capacity).

### Legal Framework

**Under E-wet 1998 (current through 2025):**
- Wind + solar installations can share a connection if:
  - Total aansluitcapaciteit (connection capacity) >= 2 MVA
  - Different parties (WOZ-objecten / WOZ objects) permitted
  - Only productie-installaties (production installations)

**Under Energiewet (from 2026):**
- Cable pooling expanded to ALL installation types:
  - Productie / opwek (production/generation)
  - Opslag (storage)
  - Conversie (conversion, e.g., elektrolysers / electrolyzers)
  - Verbruik (consumption)
- Minimum shared connection: 2 MVA
- Maximum 4 partijen (parties / WOZ-objecten) per shared connection
- Rules detailed in Energiebesluit (Energy Decree)

### ACM Vroege Toepassing (Early Adoption, November 2024)
- ACM granted permission for cable pooling with batterijen (batteries) before Energiewet enters force
- Producenten (producers), batterijsystemen (battery systems), elektrolysers, and bedrijven (businesses) can jointly use one aansluiting
- Anticipatory application of the Energiewet's cable pooling provisions

### MLOEA (Meerdere Leveranciers Op Een Aansluiting / Multiple Suppliers on One Connection)
- Related concept: multiple leveranciers (suppliers) on one aansluiting (connection)
- Enables different parties behind shared connection to have different energiecontracten (energy contracts)
- Technische meetinrichting (technical metering arrangement) required (sub-metering)
- Supported under Energiewet framework

### Cable Pooling Practical Requirements
1. **Aansluitovereenkomst (connection agreement)** with netbeheerder specifying gedeelde aansluiting (shared arrangement)
2. **Onderling contract (mutual agreement)** between pooling parties
3. **Meetinrichting (metering setup):** sub-metering for each party
4. **Profielovereenkomst (profile agreement)** with each party's energieleverancier (energy supplier)
5. **Compensatie-algoritme (compensation algorithm)** or matching algorithm for productie/verbruik-balancering (production/consumption balancing)

### Benefits
- Faster aansluiting (avoid wachtlijst for second connection)
- Lower aansluitkosten (connection costs, shared infrastructure)
- Better benutting (utilization) of existing capacity (e.g., solar peaks offset by battery storage)
- Reduced netcongestiebijdrage (grid congestion contribution)

### DEC Relevance
Cable pooling is central to DEC's co-location model: a shared aansluiting between DC (verbruik/consumption), BESS (opslag/storage), and potentially solar (opwek/generation) optimizes grid capacity use and accelerates connection timelines. See companion skill `energy-markets` for commercial structuring of cable pooling arrangements.

## 6. Private Electricity Networks

### Gesloten Distributiesysteem (GDS / Closed Distribution System)
- Privaat net (private grid) for geographically bounded area (bedrijventerrein / industrial park, campus)
- ACM-aanwijzing (ACM designation) required
- Beheerder (operator) subject to lichter reguleringsregime (lighter regulation) than public netbeheerder
- Connected users may still access openbaar net (public grid) via single aansluitpunt (connection point)
- Relevant for large campussen (campuses): datacenterparken (data center parks), kassenconcentraties (greenhouse clusters)

### Directe Lijn (Direct Line)
- Private lijn (line) connecting specific opwekker (generator) to specific verbruiker (consumer)
- No derden-toegangsplicht (third-party access requirement)
- Omgevingsvergunning (environmental permit) may be needed for kabeltracé (cable route)
- Increasingly used for on-site solar/wind to large consumer

### DEC Relevance
A GDS could be the optimal structure for a DEC campus with multiple tenants (DC, greenhouse, BESS) behind a single grid connection point. The ACM-aanwijzing process requires demonstrating geographic boundaries and industrial character. See companion skill `energy-markets` for grid connection strategy.

## 7. Energy Storage Specific

### BESS Netaansluiting (Grid Connection)
- Utility-scale BESS typically connects at HS-niveau (HV level, TenneT)
- Classified as both verbruiker (consumer) and producent (producer, bidirectional)
- Requires transportcapaciteit (transportation capacity) in both directions (afname + invoeding / offtake + feed-in)
- Cable pooling with hernieuwbare opwek (renewable generation): optimal netbenutting (grid utilization)
- TenneT TBTR contracts particularly suited for BESS (daluren laden / off-peak charging, piekuren ontladen / peak discharging)

### Markttoegang (Market Access)
- BESS needs aansluitovereenkomst AND markttoegang (market access): BRP-overeenkomst (Balancing Responsible Party agreement)
- FCR (Frequency Containment Reserve): prekwalificatie (prequalification) with TenneT required
- aFRR (automatic Frequency Restoration Reserve): prekwalificatie required
- Day-ahead and intraday markets: via energiehandelaar (energy trader) / programmaverantwoordelijke (balance responsible party)

## 8. Permits Related to Grid Infrastructure

### Bovengrondse Infrastructuur (Above-Ground Infrastructure)
- Transformatorstations (transformer stations), schakelstations (switching stations): omgevingsvergunning bouwen (Bbl / building permit)
- Geluid (noise) from transformatoren (transformers): Bal algemene regels voor geluid (Bal general rules for noise)
- Externe veiligheid (external safety) for HS-apparatuur (HV equipment): veiligheidsafstanden (safety distances)

### Ondergrondse Kabeltracés (Underground Cable Routes)
- Graafvergunning (excavation permit) from gemeente (municipality)
- WIBON/KLIC-melding (mandatory cable/pipe notification)
- Watervergunning (water permit) if crossing watergangen (waterways, water authority permit)
- Tracébesluit (route decision) for landelijk net routes (national grid routes, TenneT, major projects)

### Milieu (Environmental)
- EMV (elektromagnetische velden / electromagnetic fields): Bkl advieswaarden (advisory values) for magnetic field strength
- PCB-houdende transformatoren (PCB-containing transformers): uitfaseringsplicht (phase-out requirements)
- Oliegevulde kabels en transformatoren (oil-filled cables and transformers): bodembeschermingsmaatregelen (soil protection measures)

## 9. Typical Grid Connection Timelines

| Aansluittype (Connection Type) | Doorlooptijd (Timeline) | Bepalende Factoren (Key Factors) |
|---|---|---|
| Kleine verbruiker (small consumer, 3x25A) | 18 weken (weeks) | Standaard, wettelijke termijn (standard, regulatory deadline) |
| Middelgrote verbruiker (medium consumer, 3x80A - 10 MVA) | 6-24 maanden (months) | Capaciteitsafhankelijk (capacity dependent) |
| Grote verbruiker (large consumer, > 10 MVA, DSO) | 1-5 jaar (years) | Onderstationscapaciteit (substation capacity) |
| TenneT-aansluiting (> 110 kV) | 2-10 jaar | Netverzwaring (grid reinforcement) needed |
| BESS (flexibel contract / flexible contract) | 6-18 maanden | TenneT TBTR, sneller (faster) |
| Cable pooling-regeling (arrangement) | Additioneel 2-6 maanden | Bovenop primaire aansluitingstijd (on top of primary connection time) |
