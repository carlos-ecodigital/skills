# Gemeente Hollands Kroon: Data Center & Energy Project Permitting

## 1. Municipal Authority

### College van B&W (Mayor and Executive Board)
- Bevoegd gezag (competent authority) for omgevingsvergunningen within Hollands Kroon
- Rural gemeente in Kop van Noord-Holland (northern tip of Noord-Holland province)
- Key locations: Agriport A7 (Middenmeer), Wieringerwerf, Anna Paulowna, Hippolytushoef

### Omgevingsdienst Noord-Holland Noord (OD NHN)
- Executes Bal/Bkl enforcement in Hollands Kroon
- Milieubelastende activiteiten (environmentally impactful activities) enforcement
- Less experience with hyperscale datacenter permitting compared to OD NZKG

## 2. Agriport A7: Datacenter-Glastuinbouw Cluster

### Overview
Agriport A7 is the Netherlands' most significant example of datacenter-greenhouse co-location, making it the closest real-world analog to DEC's model.

**Current Occupants:**
- **Microsoft:** Multiple datacenter buildings (ongoing expansion controversies)
- **Google:** Large datacenter facility
- **Glastuinbouw:** Multiple large-scale greenhouse operations (tomaten / tomatoes, paprika's / bell peppers) on same terrein (site)

**Spatial Characteristics:**
- Former agricultural polder: flat, open landscape
- Large kavels (plots): 20-100+ ha available
- Mix of glastuinbouw (greenhouse) and datacenter bestemmingen (zoning designations)
- Adjacent to A7 motorway and N99
- Separated from woonkernen (residential centers) by significant afstand (distance)

### Zoning & Omgevingsplan
- Agriport A7 has dedicated bestemmingsplan / transitional omgevingsplan provisions
- Mixed agrarisch-bedrijfsmatig (agricultural-business) zoning
- Datacenters permitted in designated bedrijfskavels (business plots)
- Glastuinbouw in agrarische kavels with specific bouwvlak (building envelope) provisions
- Hoogte (height): greenhouses 8-12 m; datacenters up to 20-25 m (specific per kavel)

### Political Sensitivity
- Hyperscale datacenter development at Agriport highly politically contested
- Local opposition: concerns about energieverbruik (energy consumption), landschapsverstoring (landscape disruption), beperkte werkgelegenheid (limited employment), belastingontwijking (tax avoidance by multinationals)
- Microsoft January 2026 controversy (Amsterdam split-application strategy) refocused attention on Hollands Kroon datacenters
- Gemeenteraad (municipal council) has become increasingly critical of further datacenter expansion

## 3. DEC Model Relevance: Agriport as Archetype

### What Agriport Demonstrates
Agriport A7 is the proof-of-concept for datacenter-greenhouse proximity, but it has NOT achieved full thermal integration:
- Datacenters and greenhouses coexist spatially but operate thermally independent
- No large-scale restwarmtebenutting (waste heat utilization) from datacenters to greenhouses demonstrated at Agriport
- Greenhouses still predominantly use aardgas-WKK (natural gas CHP) for warmte and CO2
- This gap is exactly what DEC's co-location model aims to fill

### DEC Opportunity at Hollands Kroon
- **Grower proximity:** Established glastuinbouwcluster with large telers (growers) already present
- **Grid capacity:** Agriport area has substantial grid infrastructure built for existing datacenters (TenneT HS-aansluiting / HV connection present)
- **Provincial permission:** Hollands Kroon is one of three permitted datacenter gemeenten under Noord-Holland datacenterbeleid
- **CO2-voorziening (CO2 supply):** No OCAP-leiding present — CO2 gap is bigger challenge here than in Westland/Oostland
- **Warmtevraag (heat demand):** Large greenhouse area creates substantial warmtevraag year-round

### DEC Risks at Hollands Kroon
- **Political opposition:** Any new datacenter faces gemeenteraad scrutiny; must differentiate from hyperscale extractive model
- **Restwarmte skepticism:** After years of promises without delivery at Agriport, stakeholders skeptical of warmte claims
- **Limited residential warmtevraag:** Sparse bevolking (population) limits warmtenet scale beyond greenhouses
- **Grid expansion uncertainty:** Further netverzwaring (grid reinforcement) timelines unclear
- **Provincial policy evolution:** Noord-Holland datacenterbeleid may tighten further

## 4. Energie & Grid

### Grid Infrastructure at Agriport
- TenneT HS-station (HV substation) serving Agriport area
- Substantial allocated capacity for existing datacenters
- Remaining capacity uncertain — transportschaarste (grid congestion) applies
- Liander distributie (distribution) network for smaller connections

### Duurzame Energie (Sustainable Energy) in Region
- Wind energy: multiple windparken (wind farms) in Wieringermeer and coastal area
- Solar: large-scale zonneweides (solar meadows) on agrarisch land
- Cable pooling potential: wind + solar + BESS + datacenter behind shared aansluiting (connection)
- RES Noord-Holland Noord: significant zoekgebieden (search areas) for renewable energy

## 5. Stikstof (Nitrogen)

### Local Context
- Relatively low stikstofdepositiedruk (nitrogen deposition pressure) compared to Amsterdam
- BUT: Waddenzee (Wadden Sea) UNESCO World Heritage and Natura 2000 is nearby
- Duinen (dunes) along Kop van Noord-Holland coast: nitrogen-sensitive
- WKK-emissies (WKK emissions) from greenhouses: existing stikstofbron (nitrogen source)
- DC noodstroomaggregaten (emergency generators): additional NOx burden

### Natura 2000 Areas
- **Waddenzee:** Major nitrogen-sensitive area northwest of Hollands Kroon
- **Polder Westzaan:** South of Hollands Kroon
- **Noord-Hollands Duinreservaat:** West coast dunes
- AERIUS-berekening essential for any significant emitter at Agriport

## 6. Water & Soil

### Polderlandschap (Polder Landscape)
- Agriport is droogmakerij (reclaimed lake): very flat, below NAP (Amsterdam Ordnance Datum)
- Kleigrond (clay soil) with reasonable draagkracht (bearing capacity) — better than veengebied (peat area)
- Hoge grondwaterstand (high groundwater table): bronbemaling (dewatering) during bouw (construction)
- Waterbeheersing (water management): gemalen (pumping stations) maintain water level

### Waterschap
- Hoogheemraadschap Hollands Noorderkwartier (HHNK)
- Waterberging (water retention) compensation required for verharding (hardening)
- Lozing (discharge): waterschap permit for oppervlaktewater (surface water)

## 7. Building & Environment

### Bbl for Agriport
- Gevolgklasse (consequence class) assessment per datacenter building
- Brandveiligheid (fire safety): Veiligheidsregio Noord-Holland Noord advises
- BESS-installaties: PGS 37-1/37-2 applies — significant veiligheidsafstanden (safety distances)
- Landschappelijke inpassing (landscape integration): less strict than urban areas but increasingly required

### Geluid (Noise)
- Open polderlandschap: sound carries far — koelinstallaties (cooling installations) audible at distance
- Nearest gevoelige bestemmingen (sensitive uses): woningen (residences) in dorpen (villages) around Agriport
- Nachtregime (nighttime regime): Bal geluidhinder limits particularly relevant
- Cumulative geluid: multiple datacenters + greenhouses + wind turbines
