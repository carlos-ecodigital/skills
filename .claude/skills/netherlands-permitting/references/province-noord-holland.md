# Province of Noord-Holland: Data Center & Energy Project Permitting

## 1. Provincial Authority Structure

### Gedeputeerde Staten (GS / Provincial Executive)
- Bevoegd gezag (competent authority) for provincial environmental permits
- Decides on reactieve aanwijzing (reactive instruction) against municipal omgevingsplannen
- Grants ontheffing (exemption) from provinciale omgevingsverordening (provincial environmental ordinance)

### Provinciale Staten (PS / Provincial Council)
- Adopts provinciale omgevingsverordening and omgevingsvisie (provincial environmental vision)
- Sets policy frameworks: datacenterbeleid (data center policy), energietransitie (energy transition), woningbouwopgave (housing task)

### Omgevingsdienst (Environmental Enforcement Service)
- Omgevingsdienst Noordzeekanaalgebied (OD NZKG): Amsterdam, Zaanstad, Haarlemmermeer area
- Omgevingsdienst Noord-Holland Noord (OD NHN): northern part of province
- Execute Bal/Bkl inspections and handhaving (enforcement) on behalf of gemeenten and provincie

## 2. Datacenterstrategie Noord-Holland (Data Center Strategy, 2022)

### Policy Origins
Adopted by GS on 31 January 2022 in response to rapid datacenter growth in the Metropoolregio Amsterdam (Amsterdam Metropolitan Region) and Hollands Kroon.

### Key Rules

**Scope:** Applies to new datacenters with:
- Vloeroppervlak (floor area) > 2,000 m2, AND
- Elektriciteitsaansluiting (electricity connection) >= 5 MVA

**Permitted Locations:**
New datacenters meeting the scope criteria are ONLY permitted in designated bedrijventerreinen (industrial areas) within three gemeenten:
1. **Amsterdam** — Westpoort, Sloterdijk area
2. **Haarlemmermeer** — Schiphol-Rijk, Hoofddorp industrial zones
3. **Hollands Kroon** — Agriport A7, Wieringerwerf area

**Prohibited Elsewhere:**
- Provinciale omgevingsverordening explicitly restricts datacenter development outside these three gemeenten
- Gemeente cannot grant omgevingsvergunning or adopt omgevingsplan amendment for datacenter use in other locations without provincial dispensatie (dispensation)

### Restwarmte (Waste Heat) Requirement
- New datacenters must actively explore and facilitate restwarmtebenutting (waste heat utilization)
- Must demonstrate willingness to connect to nearby warmtenet (heat network) or make warmte (heat) available
- Provincial condition for permitting: plan for warmtebenutting must accompany vergunningsaanvraag (permit application)

### Verduurzaming (Sustainability) Conditions
- Energy efficiency above national minimum (PUE reporting)
- Waterverbruik (water usage) minimization — preference for luchtgekoelde systemen (air-cooled systems) over open waterkoelingssystemen (open water cooling systems)
- Circulaire economie (circular economy) commitments in bouwmaterialen (building materials)
- Ruimtelijke kwaliteit (spatial quality): landscape integration, groenvoorziening (green infrastructure)

## 3. Provinciale Omgevingsverordening (Provincial Environmental Ordinance)

### Instructieregels (Instruction Rules) for Gemeenten
The province sets instructieregels that constrain what gemeenten may allow in their omgevingsplannen:

**Relevant for DEC Projects:**
- **Bedrijventerreinen:** Province designates which terreinen (sites) are suitable for zware industrie (heavy industry) including datacenters
- **Agrarisch gebied (agricultural area):** Restrictions on non-agricultural development — relevant for greenhouse-adjacent DC projects
- **Ecologische Hoofdstructuur / Natuurnetwerk Nederland (NNN):** No new bebouwing (construction) in NNN areas
- **Landschapstype:** Requirements for landschappelijke inpassing (landscape integration) per landscape character area
- **Milieubeschermingsgebieden (environmental protection zones):** Grondwaterbeschermingsgebieden (groundwater protection areas), stiltegebieden (quiet areas)

### Reactieve Aanwijzing (Reactive Instruction)
- GS can intervene if gemeente adopts omgevingsplan that conflicts with provincial belangen (interests)
- Must be exercised within 6 weeks of omgevingsplan adoption
- Effectively blocks the conflicting plan provision
- Relevant risk: province can block datacenter zoning if it doesn't comply with datacenterbeleid

## 4. Energy Transition Policy

### RES Noord-Holland (Regionale Energiestrategie / Regional Energy Strategy)
- Two RES regions: RES Noord-Holland Noord and RES Noord-Holland Zuid
- Zoekgebieden (search areas) for wind and solar designated
- Grid capacity analysis per regio integrated with RES
- Data centers as flexible consumers considered in grid planning

### Warmtetransitievisie (Heat Transition Vision)
- Provincial coordination of gemeentelijke warmtetransitievisies (municipal heat transition plans)
- Amsterdam Warmte: Westpoort Warmte (Vattenfall) accepting datacenter restwarmte
- Hollands Kroon: limited warmtevraag (heat demand) — datacenter waste heat utilization challenging
- Haarlemmermeer: growing warmtevraag from nieuwbouwwijken (new housing developments)

## 5. Stikstof (Nitrogen) Provincial Approach

### Aanpak Stikstof Noord-Holland
- Province administers extern salderen (external offsetting) in Noord-Holland
- Provincial beleidsregel (policy rule) for extern salderen: must demonstrate reële afname (real decrease) in emissions
- Provincial verordening sets additional conditions beyond national rules
- Relevant for WKK, noodstroomaggregaten (emergency generators), verkeersbewegingen (traffic movements)

### Natura 2000 Areas in Noord-Holland
Key areas affecting development:
- Kennemerland-Zuid (dunes near Haarlem)
- Ilperveld, Varkensland, Oostzanerveld (near Amsterdam)
- Markermeer & IJmeer
- Wormer- en Jisperveld & Kalverpolder
- AERIUS-berekening required for any project with stikstofuitstoot (nitrogen emissions) near these areas

## 6. Water & Soil

### Grondwateronttrekking (Groundwater Extraction)
- Provincial bevoegdheid (competence): GS grants watervergunning for grondwateronttrekking
- Relevant for datacenter cooling, bronbemaling (dewatering) during construction
- Grondwaterbeschermingsgebieden: stricter rules near drinkwaterwinningen (drinking water extraction sites)

### Bodemsanering (Soil Remediation)
- Province coordinates bodemsanering on former industrial sites
- Relevant for brownfield datacenter development
- Historisch bodemonderzoek (historical soil survey) required before development

## 7. DEC-Specific Considerations

### Hollands Kroon: Agricultural Co-Location Opportunity
- Agriport A7: existing datacenter cluster (Microsoft, Google) adjacent to glastuinbouw
- Agricultural zoning creates both opportunity (grower proximity) and constraint (non-agricultural use restrictions)
- Province requires demonstration that datacenter component serves or complements agrarische functie (agricultural function) — waste heat utilization is key argument
- Glastuinbouwconcentratiegebied: designated greenhouse area adjacent to datacenter zone

### Amsterdam: Warmtenet Integration
- Westpoort Warmte: established warmtenet accepting restwarmte from industry
- New datacenters must negotiate warmteleveringsovereenkomst (heat supply agreement) with warmtebedrijf (heat company)
- Spatial constraints: limited expansion room in Westpoort

### Haarlemmermeer: Growing Heat Demand
- Nieuwbouwwijken (new developments) around Hoofddorp creating warmtevraag
- Potential for DC-to-residential warmtenet
- Schiphol proximity: hoogtebeperkingen (height restrictions) from luchthavenindelingsbesluit (airport zoning decree)
