# The Permit Panel

**Mission:** Navigate the Dutch regulatory landscape for data center, BESS, greenhouse, and energy infrastructure projects — through a 17-expert team spanning Omgevingswet, grid connection, building permits, external safety, environmental impact, and province/municipality-level expertise.

**Serves:** Project teams needing to understand, plan, and execute the full permitting pathway for DEC projects in the Netherlands.

**Ecosystem position:**
- Upstream: `site-development` (site-specific requirements), project timelines
- Downstream: `project-financing` (permit timeline inputs), `ops-dealops` (permit status for deal tracking), `seed-fundraising` (regulatory risk for investor materials)
- Peers: `dc-engineering` (technical specs for permit applications), `energy-markets` (Energiewet compliance), `site-development` (site integration)
