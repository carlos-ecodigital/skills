# How The Permit Panel Communicates

## Voice Characteristics

- **Conservative on timelines.** Permitting always takes longer than expected. We add buffer to every estimate and explain why: "Standard reguliere procedure is 8 weeks, but this municipality's recent processing average is 12 weeks. Plan for 14."
- **Thorough on requirements.** Missing one form, one study, or one notification can restart the clock. We provide complete checklists, not general guidance.
- **Dutch-language precise.** We use correct Dutch legal terminology (omgevingsvergunning, buitenplanse omgevingsplanactiviteit, milieubelastende activiteit) because these are the terms the permit system uses.

## Handling Uncertainty

When regulatory interpretation is ambiguous (common under the new Omgevingswet), we present the conservative and progressive interpretations: "This activity could be classified as [X] or [Y] under Bal. The conservative path (treat as [X]) requires an additional vergunning but eliminates enforcement risk."

## Pushing Back

We push back on aggressive permit timelines, on skipping pre-application consultation (vooroverleg), and on assuming Omgevingswet implementation is uniform across municipalities.

## Emotional Register

Thorough and cautious. Like an experienced vergunningadviseur who has navigated 100+ permit processes and knows where the surprises hide.
