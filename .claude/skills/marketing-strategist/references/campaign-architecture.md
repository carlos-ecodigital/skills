# Campaign Architecture — Framework for Digital Energy

Every marketing campaign follows a structured brief. This file defines the standard campaign brief template, funnel stages, and KPI framework.

---

## Campaign Brief Template

Use this template for every campaign. Fill out before producing any content or collateral.

### Campaign Brief

```
CAMPAIGN NAME: [Descriptive name, e.g., "Q2 2026 Grower Acquisition — Zuid-Holland"]

1. OBJECTIVE
   - Primary goal: [Generate X qualified leads / Sign Y LOIs / Drive Z portal signups]
   - Secondary goal: [Build awareness in segment / Test messaging / Validate channel]
   - Timeline: [Start date — End date]

2. TARGET SEGMENT
   - Persona: [Which of the 6 buyer personas]
   - Sub-segment: [Geographic, size, or behavioral filter]
   - Estimated addressable audience: [Number]
   - Key pain point to address: [One sentence]

3. THE OFFER
   - What we're proposing: [Specific offer, not generic value prop]
   - Entry action (lowest friction CTA): [E.g., "Sign up for portal" / "Book a site visit" / "Download the guide"]
   - Value equation score: DO [X/10] × PL [X/10] / TD [X/10] × ES [X/10] = [Value]

4. MESSAGING
   - Headline: [10 words max]
   - Supporting message: [25 words max]
   - Proof point: [One specific number or fact]
   - CTA: [Exact words on the button/link]

5. CHANNELS
   - Primary: [One channel]
   - Supporting: [1-2 channels]
   - Rationale: [Why these channels for this segment]

6. FUNNEL
   - Awareness: [How they first hear about this]
   - Interest: [What makes them click/engage]
   - Consideration: [What content/proof moves them forward]
   - Decision: [What triggers the conversion action]
   - Action: [The specific conversion — signup, call, LOI]

7. CONTENT REQUIRED
   - [List each piece: email sequence, LinkedIn posts, landing page, presentation, etc.]
   - Assign to: [content-engine / collateral-studio]

8. BUDGET
   - Total: EUR [X]
   - Breakdown: [Channel 1: EUR X, Channel 2: EUR X, Content production: EUR X]

9. METRICS
   - Primary KPI: [E.g., "portal signups"]
   - Target: [Number]
   - Secondary KPI: [E.g., "email reply rate"]
   - Target: [Number]
   - Reporting frequency: [Weekly / bi-weekly]

10. RISKS & CONTINGENCIES
    - Risk 1: [E.g., "Low email deliverability"] → Mitigation: [E.g., "Warm up domain first"]
    - Risk 2: [E.g., "Event cancelled"] → Mitigation: [E.g., "Shift budget to LinkedIn"]
```

## Funnel Stages — Detailed

### Stage 1: Awareness
**Goal:** Target knows DE exists and has a vague understanding of what we do.

| Tactic | Channel | Metric |
|---|---|---|
| Thought leadership posts | LinkedIn (CEO personal) | Impressions, follower growth |
| Guest articles | Trade press | Article views, website referrals |
| Event speaking | Conferences, LTO meetings | Audience size, card scans |
| Paid ads (awareness) | LinkedIn Sponsored Content | Impressions, CPM |

### Stage 2: Interest
**Goal:** Target engages with DE content and wants to learn more.

| Tactic | Channel | Metric |
|---|---|---|
| Deep-dive content | Blog articles, whitepapers | Time on page, scroll depth |
| Webinars | Virtual events | Registrations, attendance rate |
| Content downloads | Website (gated content) | Download count, email capture |
| LinkedIn engagement | Company page | Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares) |

### Stage 3: Consideration
**Goal:** Target is evaluating DE as an option and comparing to alternatives.

| Tactic | Channel | Metric |
|---|---|---|
| Case studies | Website, email, sales materials | Views, shares to colleagues |
| Site visit invitations | Direct BD | Visits scheduled, visits completed |
| Technical whitepapers | Email, direct sharing | Downloads, follow-up requests |
| Discovery calls | Phone/video | Calls completed, qualification rate |
| Proposal/presentation | Direct BD | Presentations delivered |

### Stage 4: Decision
**Goal:** Target is ready to commit and needs final reassurance.

| Tactic | Channel | Metric |
|---|---|---|
| Tailored proposal | Direct BD | Proposals sent vs. accepted |
| Reference calls | Phone (grower-to-grower, CTO-to-CTO) | References completed |
| Objection handling | Direct BD | Objections resolved |
| Commercial negotiation | Direct BD | Terms agreed |

### Stage 5: Action
**Goal:** Target converts — signs LOI, books deployment, signs contract.

| Tactic | Channel | Metric |
|---|---|---|
| Portal signup + LOI | Grower Portal | Signups, LOI signed |
| LOI/term sheet | Direct BD | LOIs/term sheets signed |
| Contract execution | Legal/BD | Contracts executed |

## Campaign Types — Templates

### Type A: Event-Based Campaign
**Duration:** 6-8 weeks (2 weeks pre, event, 4 weeks follow-up)
**Structure:**
1. **Pre-event (2 weeks):** LinkedIn posts teasing the talk topic, email invitations to known contacts, event page registration
2. **Event day:** Presentation, booth/stand, collect contacts (badge scan or QR)
3. **Post-event (1 week):** Thank-you email with presentation slides + resource link
4. **Nurture (3 weeks):** 3-email follow-up sequence; CTA: portal signup or discovery call

### Type B: Cold Outreach Campaign
**Duration:** 4-6 weeks per batch
**Structure:**
1. **List building (1 week):** Source contacts from KVK, LinkedIn, conference lists
2. **Sequence launch:** 5-email sequence over 21 days (see lead-gen-playbook.md)
3. **Response handling:** Replies routed to BD within 4 hours
4. **Positive replies → Discovery call → Qualification → Next step (site visit, portal, proposal)**

### Type C: Content-Led Inbound Campaign
**Duration:** Ongoing (minimum 90 days to see results)
**Structure:**
1. **Publish cadence:** 3-4 LinkedIn posts/week + 2 blog articles/month
2. **Lead magnet:** Gated content (grid scarcity report, heat economics calculator, market overview)
3. **Capture:** Email signup for download → auto-tagged by content interest
4. **Nurture:** Segment-specific email sequence triggered by content topic
5. **Qualify:** Engagement scoring → MQL threshold → BD outreach

### Type D: ABM (Account-Based Marketing) Campaign
**Duration:** Ongoing for target accounts
**Structure:**
1. **Account selection:** 10-20 target accounts per segment (neocloud/enterprise)
2. **Research:** Map buying committee (CTO, VP Infra, CFO, Sustainability Director)
3. **Multi-thread outreach:** LinkedIn DM + email + mutual connection introduction
4. **Personalized content:** Custom one-pager per account; custom pitch deck addressing their specific needs
5. **Trigger events:** Company announces AI investment, raises funding, posts GPU-related job → accelerate outreach
6. **Conversion:** Discovery call → site visit → proposal → contract

## KPI Dashboard Template

| KPI | Formula | Target (Grower) | Target (Neocloud) | Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Leads generated | New contacts in pipeline | 15-20/quarter | 5-10/quarter | Weekly |
| MQLs | Leads scoring >60 | 8-10/quarter | 3-5/quarter | Weekly |
| SQLs | MQLs that pass BD qualification | 4-6/quarter | 2-3/quarter | Weekly |
| LOIs/term sheets signed | Signed commitments | 2-3/quarter | 1-2/quarter | Monthly |
| CPL | Total spend / leads | <EUR 200 | <EUR 500 | Monthly |
| CPA (cost per LOI) | Total spend / LOIs | <EUR 2,000 | <EUR 5,000 | Monthly |
| Email reply rate | Replies / sent | >5% | >8% | Per sequence |
| LinkedIn engagement | (Likes+comments+shares) / impressions | >3% | >3% | Weekly |
| Pipeline velocity | Days from lead to SQL | <30 days | <45 days | Monthly |
| Funnel conversion | SQLs / Leads | >25% | >30% | Quarterly |
