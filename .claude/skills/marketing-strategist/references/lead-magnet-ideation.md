# Lead Magnet Ideation Framework

> A structured brainstorming tool for generating high-value lead magnet concepts by buyer persona. Designed to be the "divergent thinking" step between campaign strategy and collateral production.

---

## 1. When to Use This

Use this reference when:
- Designing a new campaign and need a lead capture mechanism
- A segment has low inbound volume and needs a reason to engage
- Preparing for an event and need a giveaway / follow-up resource
- Building a content marketing funnel and need a gate piece

**Workflow position:** `marketing-strategist` (campaign design) → **this ideation step** → `collateral-studio` (production)

## 3. Scoring Matrix

Score each concept on 4 dimensions to prioritize:

| Dimension | Weight | 1 (Low) | 3 (Medium) | 5 (High) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Production Effort** (inverse — lower effort = higher score) | 20% | Custom tool/app | Multi-page designed PDF | 1-page PDF, checklist, or template |
| **Perceived Value** | 30% | Generic info available elsewhere | Useful but not unique | Unique data, proprietary insight, or saves real time |
| **Buyer Journey Alignment** | 25% | Too early (awareness for a ready buyer) or too late (consideration for cold) | Matches one stage well | Perfectly timed for target stage |
| **Data Capture Quality** | 25% | Only email | Email + company + role | Email + company + role + qualifying question |

**Scoring formula:** `(Effort × 0.2) + (Value × 0.3) + (Alignment × 0.25) + (Data × 0.25) = Priority Score`

**Threshold:** Score ≥ 3.5 = build it. Score 2.5-3.5 = park for later. Score < 2.5 = skip.

| Distribution Channel | Best For | Implementation |
|---|---|---|
| **Gated landing page** | All lead magnets | Dedicated page per persona, one form, one CTA |
| **LinkedIn ad** | High-value reports, calculators | Carousel ad or single image → landing page |
| **LinkedIn post (Carlos)** | Checklists, short guides | "Comment [keyword] and I'll send it to you" |
| **Cold email attachment** | Checklists, 1-pagers | Attach to Touch 2 as "thought you'd find this useful" |
| **Event handout** | Printed checklists, infographics | Physical at conference booth + QR to digital version |
| **Newsletter CTA** | All lead magnets | Feature as CTA section in relevant segment newsletter |
| **Website pop-up/banner** | Calculator, reports | Exit intent or scroll trigger |

## 6. Lead Magnet Quality Checklist

Before publishing any lead magnet:

- [ ] **Standalone value.** Would someone share this even without DE branding?
- [ ] **One clear topic.** Not a company brochure disguised as a resource.
- [ ] **Specific to a persona.** Not "something for everyone."
- [ ] **Data is current.** All numbers, regulations, and market data verified against `de-brand-bible/references/proof-points.md` and `_shared/market-data.md`
- [ ] **Brand voice compliant.** No banned phrases, correct terminology, appropriate tone.
- [ ] **CTA inside the document.** Last page includes: website, portal link, or "book a call" — don't rely only on the landing page.
- [ ] **Bilingual version if grower-facing.** Dutch primary with English parentheticals for legal/technical terms.
- [ ] **Mobile-readable.** Large fonts, short paragraphs, works on phone screen.
