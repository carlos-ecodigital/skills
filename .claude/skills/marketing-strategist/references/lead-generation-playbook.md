# Lead Generation Playbook — Core Four for Digital Energy

Based on Alex Hormozi's $100M Leads "Core Four" framework, adapted for B2B energy infrastructure and AI colocation markets.

---

## The Core Four

| Method | Definition | Best For | DE Priority |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Warm Outreach** | Reaching out to people who already know you | Growers (referral network), partner introductions | HIGH — primary for growers |
| **Cold Outreach** | Reaching out to strangers | Neocloud CTOs, industrial energy managers | HIGH — primary for neocloud/enterprise |
| **Free Content** | Publishing expertise to attract inbound | All segments (thought leadership builds authority) | MEDIUM — build now, compounds over time |
| **Paid Ads** | Paying for targeted visibility | Neocloud/enterprise (LinkedIn), growers (trade press) | LOW initially — scale after content proves messaging |

**Resource allocation rule:**
- More time than money → Start with warm outreach + free content
- More money than time → Start with cold outreach + paid ads
- DE's current position: Early stage with strong network but limited budget → **Warm outreach + free content first, cold outreach second, paid ads last**

## Method 2: Cold Outreach

### Target Lists — By Segment

**Growers:**
- Source: KVK (Kamer van Koophandel) registry; SBI code 0111-0130 (horticulture)
- Filter: >2 hectares under glass; located in Westland, Oostland, NH, NB, Limburg
- Volume: ~2,000 qualifying operations in NL
- Contact: Director/Eigenaar (owner-director)
- Channel: Email (extracted from KVK/website) + LinkedIn + direct mail

**Neoclouds:**
- Source: GPU cloud company databases, AI infrastructure conferences attendee lists, Crunchbase/PitchBook
- Filter: Companies with >$10M raised, active GPU cloud operations, European expansion plans
- Volume: 50-100 globally; 20-30 with active European interest
- Contact: VP Infrastructure, Head of DC Ops, CTO
- Channel: LinkedIn DM + email

**Enterprises:**
- Source: Company AI/ML job postings (signal for compute investment), LinkedIn Sales Navigator
- Filter: >1,000 employees, active AI/ML teams, European HQ or operations
- Volume: 500+ in NL/EU
- Contact: CTO, VP AI/ML, Head of Infrastructure
- Channel: LinkedIn + email + analyst/partner introductions

**District Heating:**
- Source: NVDE membership, Warmtenetwerk directory, municipal energy transition plans
- Filter: Active warmtenet operators, municipalities with heat transition plans
- Volume: ~50 in NL
- Contact: Directeur, Manager Warmte
- Channel: Direct BD + industry events (not cold email — too relationship-driven)

### Cold Email Sequence Framework

**Sequence structure: 5 emails over 21 days**

| Email | Day | Subject Type | Content Focus | CTA |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Day 0 | Pain-first | Name the specific problem they face | Reply to explore |
| 2 | Day 3 | Proof | Share a relevant data point or case | Reply or click to resource |
| 3 | Day 7 | Value | Specific benefit with numbers | Book a 15-min call |
| 4 | Day 14 | Social proof | What similar companies are doing | Book a call |
| 5 | Day 21 | Break-up | Honest close; leave door open | Reply if timing changes |

**Rules:**
- Under 150 words per email body
- One CTA per email (not multiple)
- Personalize the first line (company name, specific situation, recent news)
- No attachments in cold emails (deliverability risk)
- Send from personal email addresses, not marketing@ or info@

See `content-engine/examples/cold-email-sequences.md` for fully written sequences per segment.

## Method 3: Free Content

### Content Pillars (Thought Leadership)

| Pillar | Topics | Target Audience | Format |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Grid Scarcity** | Dutch transportschaarste, 60 GW queue, cable pooling, Energiewet | All segments | LinkedIn posts, blog articles, event talks |
| **Waste Heat Economics** | Heat recovery math, gas vs. DEC comparison, ETS2 impact, Wcw reform | Growers, district heating, industrial | LinkedIn, grower trade press, presentations |
| **Sovereign AI** | EU AI Act, data sovereignty, European compute gap, hyperscaler dependency | Neocloud, enterprise, institution | LinkedIn, tech press, conference talks |
| **Project Finance** | Infrastructure as asset class, non-recourse debt, pension fund investment | Investors, energy partners | LinkedIn, investor events, whitepapers |
| **BESS + AI** | Revenue stacking, cable pooling, BESS-as-bridge-to-DC model | Energy partners, BESS investors | LinkedIn, energy industry events |

### Content Cadence

| Channel | Frequency | Owner | Content Type |
|---|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn (CEO personal) | 3-4x/week | CEO writes (or approves) | Insights, deal learnings, market observations |
| LinkedIn (company page) | 2-3x/week | Marketing | Company news, data points, event promotion |
| Blog / website | 2x/month | Marketing + technical team | Deep dives, how-we-did-it, market analysis |
| Newsletter | Monthly | Marketing | Curated: 1 original article + 3 industry news + 1 CTA |
| Guest articles | Quarterly | CEO / CTO | Trade press (Energeia, DCD, Data Center Knowledge) |

### Content-to-Lead Conversion

Free content generates leads when it includes a clear next step:
- LinkedIn post → "Comment [keyword] and I'll send you [resource]"
- Blog article → Email capture for extended report / data set
- Conference talk → "Scan QR for site visit invitation"
- Newsletter → Segment by click behavior → trigger relevant cold sequence

## Method 4: Paid Ads

### When to Start Paid
- AFTER free content has validated messaging (which topics get engagement)
- AFTER cold outreach has validated offer (which sequences convert)
- Minimum budget: EUR 3,000-5,000/month per channel for statistical significance
- Start with one channel, prove ROI, then expand

### Channel Recommendations

| Channel | Target Segment | Ad Type | Budget Start |
|---|---|---|---|
| **LinkedIn Ads** | Neocloud (VP Infra), Enterprise (CTO) | Sponsored content + lead gen forms | EUR 5,000/mo |
| **Google Ads** | Neocloud, enterprise | Search: "colocation Netherlands," "GPU hosting Europe," "data center NL" | EUR 3,000/mo |
| **Trade press ads** | Growers | Onder Glas, Vakblad voor de Bloemisterij, Kas Magazine | EUR 1,000-2,000/issue |
| **LinkedIn Ads** | District heating | Sponsored content targeting warmtenet professionals | EUR 3,000/mo |

### LinkedIn Ads — Targeting Parameters
- **Neocloud/Enterprise:** Job titles: VP Infrastructure, Head of Data Center, CTO, VP AI. Company size: 50-5000. Industry: Technology, Cloud Computing. Geography: EU + US/UK.
- **Growers:** Job titles: Director, Eigenaar. Industry: Agriculture, Greenhouse. Geography: NL only. Supplement with matched audiences from KVK list.
- **District Heating:** Job titles: Director, Manager Warmte, Technical Director. Company: known utility names (Ennaturlijk, Vattenfall, etc.). Geography: NL.

## Lead Qualification Framework

### Scoring Model

| Signal | Points | Segment Applicability |
|---|---|---|
| Has secured grid connection >5 MW | +30 | Grower, energy partner |
| Located in NL (not Amsterdam) | +20 | All |
| Annual gas spend >EUR 500K | +20 | Grower, industrial |
| Active GPU cloud operations | +25 | Neocloud |
| European expansion announced | +20 | Neocloud, enterprise |
| AI/ML team >10 people | +15 | Enterprise |
| Sustainability mandate / ESG reporting | +10 | All |
| Engaged with content (2+ touchpoints) | +10 | All |
| Responded to outreach | +15 | All |

### Qualification Thresholds
| Score | Status | Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0-30 | Cold lead | Nurture with content |
| 31-60 | Warm lead | Trigger outreach sequence |
| 61-80 | Marketing qualified (MQL) | Hand to BD for qualification call |
| 81+ | Sales qualified (SQL) | Schedule discovery meeting |
