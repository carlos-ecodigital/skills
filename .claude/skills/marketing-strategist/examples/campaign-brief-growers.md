# Campaign Brief: Q2 2026 Grower Acquisition — Zuid-Holland / Westland

## 1. OBJECTIVE
- **Primary:** Generate 15 qualified grower leads and 3 signed LOIs in Q2 2026
- **Secondary:** Establish DE presence in Westland/Oostland greenhouse corridor
- **Timeline:** 1 April — 30 June 2026

## 2. TARGET SEGMENT
- **Persona:** Grower (Teler) — Dutch greenhouse operator
- **Sub-segment:** Westland and Oostland (Zuid-Holland), >5 hectares under glass, annual gas spend >EUR 500K
- **Estimated addressable:** ~400 operations matching criteria (KVK data)
- **Key pain point:** Gas costs are 30-50% of operating expenses and rising. ETS2 makes it worse from 2027.

## 3. THE OFFER
- **Proposition:** "We place an AI data center on your land. You receive free waste heat. Your gas bill drops 60-80%. No investment required from you."
- **Entry action:** Sign up on the Grower Portal (30 seconds, email only)
- **Value equation:**
  - Dream Outcome: 9/10 (60-80% gas cost reduction + passive heat revenue)
  - Perceived Likelihood: 6/10 (new concept — needs proof; pilot not yet operational)
  - Time Delay: 5/10 (LOI in 10 days, but heat delivery in 18-30 months)
  - Effort/Sacrifice: 9/10 (zero CAPEX, 30-sec signup, pre-filled forms)
  - **Value Score: (9 × 6) / (5 × 1) = 10.8** — Strong, but perceived likelihood is the weak link

## 4. MESSAGING

### Dutch (primary)
- **Headline:** "Uw gasrekening 60-80% omlaag — zonder investering"
- **Supporting:** "Wij plaatsen een AI-datacenter op uw terrein. U ontvangt gratis restwarmte."
- **Proof point:** "In Nederland wacht 60 GW aan aanvragen op een netaansluiting. Uw bestaande aansluiting is goud waard."
- **CTA:** "Meld u aan — het kost 30 seconden"

### English (secondary, for advisors)
- **Headline:** "Cut your gas bill 60-80% — zero investment"
- **Supporting:** "We co-locate an AI data center on your property. You receive free waste heat."
- **Proof point:** "60 GW of grid connection applications queued in the Netherlands. Your existing connection is a scarce asset."
- **CTA:** "Sign up — it takes 30 seconds"

## 6. FUNNEL

```
AWARENESS (400 growers in Westland/Oostland)
    ↓ LTO events (3-5 per event) + cold email (5% reply rate = 10)
    ↓ LinkedIn content reaches 500+ in agriculture
INTEREST (30-40 engaged)
    ↓ Reply to email or approach at event
    ↓ Receive one-pager + portal link
CONSIDERATION (15-20 portal signups)
    ↓ Portal pre-fills company data from KVK
    ↓ DE Admin reviews and qualifies
DECISION (8-10 qualified)
    ↓ Phone call / site visit from BD
    ↓ Walk through economics with their advisor
ACTION (3 LOIs signed)
    ↓ LOI generated in portal, e-signed
```

## 10. RISKS & CONTINGENCIES

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| LTO events cancelled or poorly attended | Medium | Loss of primary channel | Shift to direct BD calling + increase email volume to 300 |
| Low email deliverability (new domain) | Medium | Reduced email reach | Warm up domain 2 weeks pre-launch; use existing personal emails initially |
