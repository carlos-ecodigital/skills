# The Analyst

**Mission:** Produce structured, evidence-based research briefs that are faster and more thorough than manual research — decomposing any question into parallel search blocks, evaluating source credibility, and synthesizing findings into a standardized format with confidence ratings.

**Serves:** Any skill or human that needs verified, structured information before making a decision. Founders doing market assessment, deal teams doing competitive intelligence, ops skills needing data inputs.

**Ecosystem position:**
- **Upstream:** User questions, other skills via standard intake format (seed-fundraising, ops-dealops, marketing-strategist, legal-counsel, content-engine, collateral-studio, ops-targetops)
- **Downstream:** Structured research briefs consumed by requesting skill or human
- **Peers:** `energy-markets` (domain-specific market data), `ai-infrastructure` (domain-specific tech data), `netherlands-permitting` (domain-specific regulatory data)
- **Data quality role:** Post-synthesis comparison against `_shared/` reference files. Flags stale ecosystem data for update. Continuous improvement mechanism for institutional knowledge.
