# Content Calendar Framework

Recommended publishing cadence, thematic pillars, and seasonal hooks for Digital Energy.

---

## Weekly Cadence

| Day | Channel | Content Type | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monday | LinkedIn (CEO) | Week's insight / industry observation | CEO |
| Tuesday | LinkedIn (company) | Data point or market stat | Marketing |
| Wednesday | LinkedIn (CEO) | Story / deal learning / behind-the-scenes | CEO |
| Wednesday | Blog | Deep-dive article (2x/month) | Marketing |
| Thursday | LinkedIn (company) | Event promotion or team highlight | Marketing |
| Thursday | LinkedIn (CEO) | Thought leadership / opinion | CEO |
| Friday | Newsletter (1x/month) | Curated monthly digest | Marketing |

## Monthly Themes

Rotate through five content pillars. Each month emphasizes one pillar while maintaining presence across all.

| Month | Primary Pillar | Supporting Content |
|---|---|---|
| January | **Regulatory outlook** (Wcw, ETS2, Energiewet predictions for the year) | Market data roundup from prior year |
| February | **Grid scarcity** (TenneT updates, queue stats, cable pooling) | Neocloud capacity messaging |
| March | **Grower economics** (pre-growing-season heat cost focus) | Seasonal: growers planning next year's energy |
| April | **Sovereign AI** (EU AI Act, data sovereignty, European compute gap) | Conference season content |
| May | **BESS + Energy** (revenue stacking, arbitrage, ancillary services) | Spring market data updates |
| June | **Heat + Sustainability** (ESG reporting season, ETS2 timeline) | District heating focus |
| July | **Mid-year review** (pipeline progress, market developments) | Lower-frequency: summer scheduling |
| August | **Technical deep-dives** (cooling, PUE, power density) | Neocloud/enterprise technical content |
| September | **Prinsjesdag** (Dutch government budget, energy policy changes) | Regulatory analysis |
| October | **Conference season** (OCP, GTC, energy events) | Event-driven content |
| November | **Investment thesis** (year-end financial planning, investor outreach) | Institutional investor focus |
| December | **Year in review + predictions** | Light content, relationship maintenance |

## Seasonal Hooks (NL/Energy-specific)

| Date / Period | Hook | Content Angle |
|---|---|---|
| Prinsjesdag (3rd Tue Sep) | Annual government budget and energy policy | Policy analysis post, regulatory implications |
| LTO Glaskracht events (various) | Grower sector meetings | Pre/post event content, grower-focused |
| EU ETS Phase changes | Carbon price milestones | Cost impact analysis for gas users |
| TenneT capacity reports (quarterly) | Grid congestion data updates | Data-driven posts on scarcity |
| Wcw implementation dates | Heat network regulation milestones | District heating compliance content |
| EPEX SPOT price events | Extreme price spreads, negative hours | BESS revenue opportunity content |
| Conference dates (GTC, OCP, SC) | AI infrastructure events | Pre/post conference thought leadership |
| Growing season start (Feb-Mar) | Growers commit energy budgets | Timely outreach, heat cost comparison |
| Growing season end (Oct-Nov) | Post-season cost review | "How much did gas cost you this year?" |
