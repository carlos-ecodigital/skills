# Press Release Template

---

## Standard Format

```
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

[HEADLINE — Factual, specific, no superlatives]
[e.g., "Digital Energy Secures 25.5 MW Grid Connection for AI Data Center
and Battery Storage in Noord-Holland"]

[SUBHEADING — One line of supporting context]
[e.g., "Project combines AI colocation with waste heat supply to adjacent
greenhouse operations"]

[City], [Date] — Digital Energy Group AG ("Digital Energy") today announced
[what happened, in one sentence].

[LEAD PARAGRAPH: Expand on what, when, where, why. Include the most
important number. 2-3 sentences max.]

[CEO QUOTE]
"[First-person statement from CEO. Should add perspective, not repeat
facts. Focus on why this matters for the market or the partners.]"
said Carlos Reuven, CEO of Digital Energy Group.

[SUPPORTING PARAGRAPH 1: Technical or commercial details. Grid capacity,
timeline, partnership details, facility specifications.]

[SUPPORTING PARAGRAPH 2: Market context. Why this matters — grid scarcity,
regulatory tailwinds, market demand. Include 1-2 sourced data points.]

[OPTIONAL PARTNER QUOTE: If a partner (Lenovo, grower, utility) is named,
include their quote here.]

[NEXT STEPS: What happens next — timeline, development milestones.]

###

ABOUT DIGITAL ENERGY

Digital Energy Group AG (Zug, Switzerland) develops sovereign AI
infrastructure across Europe. The company co-locates AI data centers on
industrial and agricultural sites, recovering waste heat for greenhouses,
district heating networks, and industrial users. Each project is
structured as a non-recourse financed Dutch BV with integrated battery
energy storage. Digital Energy's development pipeline includes multiple
projects in the Netherlands, supported by partnerships with Lenovo and
Nokia for hardware and network infrastructure.
For more information, visit [website].

MEDIA CONTACT
[Name]
[Title]
[Email]
[Phone]
```

---

## Press Release Checklist

- [ ] Headline is factual and specific (no "pleased to announce," "game-changing," etc.)
- [ ] Lead paragraph answers: who, what, when, where, why
- [ ] CEO quote adds perspective beyond the facts
- [ ] At least one sourced data point for market context
- [ ] No confidential commercial terms (pricing, equity splits, internal projections)
- [ ] Partner named only with their approval
- [ ] Boilerplate is current (check against `de-brand-bible/examples/boilerplate-paragraphs.md`)
- [ ] NL version prepared if targeting Dutch media (not a direct translation — adapt emphasis)
- [ ] Contact information is correct and responsive
