# Investor Landscape -- Infrastructure / AI / Energy Seed Rounds

## 1. Investor Taxonomy

| Type | Check Size (Seed) | Decision Speed | What They Want | How to Reach Them |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Seed VC | EUR 0.5-5M | 2-6 weeks | Growth, team, market, moat | Warm intro, demo day, cold email |
| Climate tech VC | EUR 0.5-5M | 3-8 weeks | Impact + returns, technology | Conferences, warm intro, thesis alignment |
| Infrastructure fund | EUR 5-50M (usually Series A+) | 2-6 months | Cash yield, contracted revenue, asset quality | Advisor introduction, institutional network |
| Family office | EUR 0.5-10M | 2-8 weeks | Alignment with values, relationship, deal terms | Personal network, advisor, conferences |
| Strategic investor | EUR 1-10M | 4-12 weeks | Market access, technology, synergy | Direct BD, partnerships, conferences |
| Angel / syndicate | EUR 25K-500K | 1-4 weeks | Founder quality, early entry, upside | Angel networks, LinkedIn, personal network |
| Government / DFI | EUR 1-10M | 3-12 months | Policy objectives, job creation, sovereignty | RVO, Invest-NL, EIB direct application |
| Pension fund / insurer | EUR 10-100M (usually later stage) | 6-12 months | Yield, duration match, ESG | Advisor, fund placement agent |

## 3. Investor Targeting Strategy

### 3.1 The Funnel

| Stage | Target Count | Conversion | Output |
|---|---|---|---|
| Long list | 150-200 investors | -- | Spreadsheet with all potential |
| Researched fit | 80-100 investors | 50-65% | Confirmed sector + stage + check size fit |
| Outreach sent | 60-80 investors | 75-80% of researched | Personalized outreach |
| First meeting | 30-40 meetings | ~50% of outreach | Average seed round requires 39 meetings |
| Second meeting | 12-18 meetings | 30-45% of first meetings | Serious interest |
| DD / term sheet | 3-6 investors | 20-50% of second meetings | Active negotiation |
| Closed | 1-3 investors | -- | Lead + co-investors |

### 3.2 Tier System

| Tier | Definition | Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 (5-10 investors) | Dream investors; perfect thesis alignment | Warm intro only; prepare extensively |
| Tier 2 (15-25 investors) | Strong fit; active in sector and stage | Warm intro preferred; cold OK if personalized |
| Tier 3 (30-50 investors) | Good fit; may need education on sector | Mix of warm and cold; efficient outreach |
| Practice (5-10 investors) | Lower priority; use to practice pitch | Schedule before Tier 1 meetings |

### 3.3 Investor Research Template

For each target investor, document:
- Fund name and relevant partner
- Investment thesis (from website/blog)
- Recent investments in adjacent space
- Check size range and stage preference
- Portfolio conflicts (have they invested in a competitor?)
- Connection path (mutual contact, conference, cold)
- Why they're a fit (specific, not generic)

## 4. Outreach Strategy

### 4.1 Warm Introduction Flow

1. Identify mutual connection (LinkedIn, portfolio company founder, advisor)
2. Ask connection for a specific, forwardable intro email
3. Provide connection with a 3-sentence blurb they can forward
4. Follow up within 24 hours of intro
5. Share deck after first response, not before

### 4.2 Cold Outreach Principles

- Subject line: specific + relevant ("BESS + AI data center on Dutch grid connections")
- First line: why THIS investor (reference their thesis, portfolio, or recent content)
- Second line: what you do (one sentence, no jargon)
- Third line: traction proof (strongest metric)
- Fourth line: ask (15-minute call or "happy to send the deck")
- No attachments in cold email (link to DocSend)

### 4.3 Follow-Up Cadence

| Day | Action |
|---|---|
| Day 0 | Initial outreach |
| Day 3 | If no response: brief follow-up with new data point |
| Day 7 | If no response: one more attempt with different angle |
| Day 14 | If no response: move to "revisit in 3 months" list |
| After meeting | Same-day thank you + materials promised |
| Post-meeting +3 days | Check-in with additional information |
| Monthly | Investor update email to all engaged investors |

## 5. Investor Update Best Practice

Once you have investors in your pipeline, send monthly updates to maintain momentum:

| Section | Content |
|---|---|
| Headline | One sentence: biggest win this month |
| Key metrics | 3-5 numbers (MW, revenue, customers, pipeline) |
| Progress | What you achieved this month |
| Challenges | One honest challenge and how you're addressing it |
| Ask | Specific help needed (intros, expertise, hiring) |
| Looking ahead | Next month's priorities |
