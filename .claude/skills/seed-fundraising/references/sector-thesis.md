# Sector Thesis -- Digital Infrastructure, AI & Energy

## 1. The Convergence Thesis

Three mega-trends are converging to create a once-in-a-generation infrastructure investment opportunity:

1. **AI compute demand** is growing exponentially, requiring massive new data center capacity
2. **Grid congestion** in the Netherlands and Europe makes existing power connections extremely valuable
3. **Energy transition regulation** (Wcw, Energiewet, EU ETS2) creates mandatory demand for waste heat and storage

Companies positioned at this intersection -- co-locating AI compute, energy storage, and heat recovery on existing grid infrastructure -- capture value from all three trends simultaneously.

### 3.2 Revenue Stacking Economics

The platform thesis: combining multiple revenue streams on one infrastructure platform generates returns unavailable from any single asset class.

| Revenue Stack | Standalone IRR | Integrated Platform IRR | Delta |
|---|---|---|---|
| BESS only (merchant) | 8-12% | -- | -- |
| DC only (colocation) | 10-14% | -- | -- |
| Heat only | 4-8% | -- | -- |
| BESS + DC + Heat (integrated) | -- | 15-22%+ | +5-10% from synergies |

Synergy sources:
- Shared grid connection (no incremental connection cost)
- Shared land/site (reduced per-MW cost)
- Heat revenue is incremental with zero fuel cost
- BESS provides grid services that benefit DC power quality
- Integrated offering differentiates from pure-play competitors

## 7. Investment Thesis Summary

**The thesis in one paragraph:**

European AI sovereignty requires European compute infrastructure. Grid congestion in the Netherlands makes existing power connections a non-replicable asset. The convergence of AI compute demand, battery storage economics, and mandatory heat transition regulation creates a platform opportunity where integrated developers capture outsized returns. Companies that co-locate AI data centers, battery storage, and waste heat recovery on existing grid connections in congested markets will generate multiple revenue streams from a single infrastructure platform, with moats that strengthen over time.
