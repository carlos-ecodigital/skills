## Writing Checklist

- [ ] 400-600 words (one-page) or 800-1,000 words (two-page)
- [ ] First sentence is the strongest claim
- [ ] Every number is specific (not "significant" or "substantial")
- [ ] All market data sourced and current (<18 months)
- [ ] Instrument and amount stated clearly
- [ ] Milestones with dates, not vague timelines
- [ ] Confidential marking present
- [ ] Formatted to fit on one A4/Letter page (10-11pt, 2cm margins)
- [ ] PDF renders correctly across devices
- [ ] Contact information complete
