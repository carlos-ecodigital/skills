## 4. Investor-Type Adaptations

### 4.1 Seed VC

| Element | Emphasis |
|---|---|
| Subject line | Growth + market size |
| Opening | Your growth rate or strongest traction metric |
| Body focus | Market opportunity, team, moat |
| Ask | Clean terms, quick process |
| Tone | Altman + Lochmiller: direct, proof-first |

**Example subject**: "25.5 MW grid access in 10-year queue market -- EUR 5M seed"

### 4.2 Climate / Infrastructure VC

| Element | Emphasis |
|---|---|
| Subject line | Sector thesis alignment |
| Opening | Reference their investment thesis or portfolio |
| Body focus | Technology differentiation, regulatory tailwinds, ESG metrics |
| Ask | Thesis fit, partnership value |
| Tone | Wood + Liebreich: data-driven, thesis-aligned |

**Example subject**: "Wright's Law meets grid congestion: integrated BESS + AI DC platform"

### 4.3 Family Office

| Element | Emphasis |
|---|---|
| Subject line | Mission alignment + return profile |
| Opening | Values alignment, personal connection |
| Body focus | Impact narrative, risk-adjusted returns, founder story |
| Ask | Relationship-building, long-term alignment |
| Tone | Liebreich + Musk: mission with evidence |

**Example subject**: "European energy sovereignty + 15-22% platform IRR"

### 4.4 Infrastructure Fund

| Element | Emphasis |
|---|---|
| Subject line | Cash yield + asset quality |
| Opening | Contracted revenue, asset description |
| Body focus | Unit economics, contracted vs. merchant split, construction risk |
| Ask | Institutional process, co-investment structure |
| Tone | Liebreich: institutional-grade, measured |

**Example subject**: "EUR X revenue-generating BESS + DC assets on existing NL grid"

### 4.5 Angel / Syndicate

| Element | Emphasis |
|---|---|
| Subject line | Founder story + early access |
| Opening | Personal conviction, why you're building this |
| Body focus | Founder-market fit, early entry opportunity, clean terms |
| Ask | Quick decision, small check welcome |
| Tone | Musk + Altman: vision + clean terms |

**Example subject**: "Turning wasted data center heat into greenhouse revenue"

### 4.6 Strategic Investor

| Element | Emphasis |
|---|---|
| Subject line | Synergy + market access |
| Opening | How partnership creates mutual value |
| Body focus | Technology integration, commercial partnership, market access |
| Ask | Investment + partnership exploration |
| Tone | Lochmiller: win-win-win framing |

**Example subject**: "Colocation partnership: grid access + heat revenue at Dutch industrial sites"

## 6. Email Hygiene Rules

- [ ] Personalized first line referencing THIS investor (not generic)
- [ ] No attachments in first cold email (use DocSend link)
- [ ] Total cold email under 150 words / 5 sentences
- [ ] Clear call-to-action (15-minute call or "send the deck")
- [ ] Professional signature with phone and email
- [ ] No "Hope this finds you well" or generic openers
- [ ] Subject line under 60 characters, specific to your company
- [ ] BCC yourself for CRM tracking
- [ ] Send between Tuesday-Thursday, 8:00-10:00 AM recipient's timezone
- [ ] Track opens/clicks if using email tracking (SalesHandy, Mixmax, HubSpot)
